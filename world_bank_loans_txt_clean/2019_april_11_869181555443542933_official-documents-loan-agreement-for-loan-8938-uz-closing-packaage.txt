SCHEDULE 1 

Project Description 

The objective of the Project is to support the expansion of rural enterprise activity 

and job creation in the Ferghana Valley. 

The Project consists of the following parts: 

Part 1. Enterprise Development 

Support of micro, small, and medium size enterprise development in the Ferghana 
Valley by: 

(a) facilitating business incubation and mentorship by supporting: (i) a 

business incubator hub program for rural entrepreneurs; (ii) activities for launching 

innovation start-ups; (iii) regional innovation centers; and (iv) development of a rural 

apprenticeship pilot program; 

(b) technical assistance to implement demand-driven investment proposals to 

strengthen value chains and expand or establish enterprise clusters in the agriculture, food 

processing, handicraft, rural services, tourism, and small manufacturing sectors to expand 

productive activities or reach new markets; and 

(c) supporting market promotion and technology testing activities in order to 

facilitate the launching of enterprise clusters, or small and medium enterprise activities. 

Part 2. Access to Finance 

Facilitating access to finance by micro, small, and medium size enterprises in the 

Ferghana Valley by: 

(a) provision of the Sub-loans in the agriculture, food processing, handicraft, 

rural services, tourism, and small manufacturing sectors; and strengthening the institutional 

capacity of the PFIs to provide the Sub-loans; 

(b) strengthening the SFSED capacity to issue partial credit guarantees 

through: (i) institutional capacity building of the SFSED; and (11) provision of funds for 

partial credit guarantees issued or to be issued by the SFSED in the agriculture, food 
processing, handicraft, rural services, tourism, and small manufacturing sectors; and 

(c) support of pilot financial services proposals by financial institutions.
Part 3. Project Management and Coordination 

Support to the Agency and other entities of the Borrower for Project management, 

implementation, and monitoring, evaluation, and reporting, including in the areas of 

financial management, procurement, disbursement, and social and environmental 

protection.

Section I. Implementation Arrangements 

A. Institutional Arrangements 

1. Throughout Project implementation, the Borrower shall maintain the SC with 

structure, functions and responsibilities necessary and appropriate to facilitate Project 

coordination and support. 

2. Throughout Project implementation, the Borrower shall maintain the Agency, and 

within the Agency establish and maintain the PIU, both with staff, resources, and functions 

and terms of reference necessary and appropriate for Project management, implementation, 

monitoring, evaluation, and reporting, as set out in the POM, and satisfactory to the Bank. 

B. Project Implementation 

1. The Borrower shall prepare and adopt the POM, the Credit Line Guidelines, the 

Partial Credit Guarantee Guidelines, and Safeguard Documents, all in accordance with the 

terms and conditions of this Agreement, and satisfactory to the Bank. 

2: The Project shall be implemented in accordance with the terms and conditions of: 

(a) this Agreement; and (b) the POM, the Safeguard Documents, the Credit Line 

Guidelines, and the Partial Credit Guarantee Guidelines, which shall not be amended, 

suspended, repealed or waived without the prior approval of the Bank. In case of any 

conflict between the terms and conditions of this Agreement and the document(s) referred 

to in paragraph | (b), this Agreement shall prevail. 

3. Without limitation of Section I, Part B, paragraph 2 of this Agreement, the 

Borrower shall ensure that: 

(a) the Agency shall have the overall Project management, implementation, 

monitoring, evaluation, and reporting responsibilities; 

(b) Part 1 (a) of the Project shall be implemented by the Agency, with participation of 
the Mol, the CCI, and other entities, as appropriate; 

(c) Part 1 (b) of the Project shall be implemented by the Agency, with participation of 
other agencies and entities, as appropriate; 

(d) Part 1 (c) of the Project shall be implemented by the Agency; 

(e) Part 2 (a) of the Project shall be implemented by the Agency and the PFIs;
(f) Part 2 (b) of the Project shall be implemented by the Agency and the SFSED; 

(g) Part 2 (c) of the Project shall be implemented by the Agency and the PFIs; and 

(h) Part 3 of the Project shall be implemented by the Agency.
