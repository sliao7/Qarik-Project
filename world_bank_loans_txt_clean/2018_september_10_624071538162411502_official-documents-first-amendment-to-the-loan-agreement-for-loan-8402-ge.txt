Georgia: Third Secondary and Local Roads Project 
(Loan No. 8402-GE) 

First Amendment to the Loan Agreement 

We refer to the Loan Agreement dated July 9, 2014, for the above-referenced Project (“Agreement”) 
between Georgia (“Borrower”) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development 
(“Bank”), and to the letter from the Borrower dated May 11, 2018, requesting certain amendments to 
the Agreement. 

We are pleased to amend the Agreement as follows: 

1. SCHEDULE 1: Project Description 

Part A.1 is amended and replaced in its entirety to read as follows: 

“1, Rehabilitation and improvement of about 269 km of secondary and local roads, including: (a) 
about 47 km of Jocal roads; and (b) about 222 km of secondary roads including 15 km of the Gurjaani- 
Bakurtsikhe bypass.” 

Part A.2 is amended and replaced in its entirety to read as follows: 

“2. Preparing detailed designs of about 210 km of roads and conducting construction supervision and 
quality assurance services for all civil works financed under the Project.” 

Part B.2 is amended and replaced in its entirety to read as follows: 

2, Carrying out of Training and/or a study tour on optimization of planning and budgeting for the 
Borrower’s municipalities that are in charge of management of local road assets.” 

Part C is amended and replaced in its entirety to read as follows: 

“Developing and implementing road safety measures on secondary and focal roads through the 
implementation of additional road safety engineering measures including sidewalks, pedestrian 

crossing, installation of speed bumps, barriers, blinking traffic lights near schools and other public 
places outside the scope of rehabilitation or improvement works.”
