Re: Loan No. 7523-BR (Teresina Enhancing Municipal Governance 
and Quality of Life Project — Programa Lagoas do Norte) 

Amendment to Loan Agreement 

Excellency: 

I refer to the Loan Agreement entered into between the International Bank for 

Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the Municipality of Teresina (the Borrower) 

dated July 24, 2008 (the Loan Agreement) for the above-captioned Project. Capitalized terms 

used in this letter (Amendment Letter) and not defined herein have the meaning ascribed to them 

in the Loan Agreement. 

I am pleased to inform you that pursuant to the communication from the Secretaria do 

Tesouro Nacional to the Bank dated March 1, 2013, the Ministério da Fazenda has agreed to 

COFIEX’s recommendation to have the Loan Agreement amended to extend the closing date to 

December 31, 2014, and partially restructure the Project. The Bank hereby agrees to amend the 

Loan Agreement to extend the closing date and partially restructure the Project. Accordingly: 

1. Part | of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is amended to delete subparagraphs (iii) and 

(v) and revise subparagraph (iv), all to read as follows: 

Part 1: Municipal Management Modernization, City Development, and Project 

Management 

Strengthening the Borrower’s management and technical capacity, through the 

provision of technical assistance and equipment for, inter alia: 

(i) consolidating and improving the efficiency of the Borrower’s financial and budget 

management and planning capacity; 

(ii) modernizing the water supply and sanitation service delivery to comply with the 

WSS Law, including, inter alia: (a) the preparation and dissemination of a 

municipal integrated water supply and sanitation plan; (b) the creation and 
strengthening of a municipal water supply and sanitation regulating body; and (c) 

the formalization of the water supply and sanitation service delivery mechanism for 

the Borrower’s territory consistent with the WSS Law; 

—— 
v

(iii) deleted and left intentionally blank, 

(iv) modernizing the Borrower’s urban and environmental management, including the 

preparation of the Borrower’s municipal urban and transport management master 

plans and the elaboration of new or updated laws to accompany the finalization of 

these plans; 

(v) deleted and left intentionally blank; and 

(vi) carrying out Project management, monitoring and evaluation, to support the PMU 

to carry out the procurement, financial management, environmental, social and 

other requirements of the Project. 

2. Section [V.B of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read as follows: 

The Closing Date is December 31, 2014. The Bank will only grant an extension of the 

Closing Date after the Guarantor’s Ministry of Finance has informed the Bank that it 

agrees with such extension. 

If you agree with the foregoing amendment, please countersign and date the two copies 
of this Amendment Letter in the space provided below, and return one countersigned copy to us. 
Upon the Bank’s receipt of the countersigned copy of this Amendment Letter, this Amendment 

Letter will become effective as of the date of your countersignature.
