SCHEDULE 1 

Project Description 

The objective of this Project is to reduce water pollutant releases from crop and livestock 
production in selected areas of Guangdong Province. 

Part 1

(b) 

(c) 

(d) 

The Project consists of the following parts: 

Environmentally Friendly Crop Production 

Soil Nutrient Management 

(i) Development and dissemination of sound soil nutrient management plans 
through soil nutrient sampling and analysis. 

(ii) Promotion, through the provision of Subsidies, of use of various technical 
measures to improve application and utilization efficiencies of fertilizers based 
on soil nutrient management plans including: (A) application of formula 
fertilizers; (B) application of slow-release fertilizers; (C) development of 
fertigation facilities in vegetable and orchard farms; and (D) adoption of three 
control techniques on rice paddies. 

Integrated Pest Management (PM) 

(i) Upgrading of pest monitoring and early warning systems; and 

(ii) promotion of integrated pest management practices for key cropping systems 
through the provision of Subsidies for professional pest management services, 
and for the acquisition of pesticides and related equipment. 

Conservation Agriculture Pilots 

Provision of CA Pilot Sub-grants to farmers for the carrying out of CA Pilots to 
demonstrate the use of no-till or limited tillage technologies and practices for typical 
cropping systems. 

Implementation Support to Beneficiaries 

(i) Provision of field technica] support to Beneficiaries, in particular smallholders, in 
crop production practices promoted by the Project; 

(ii) Provision of Sub-grants to eligible Village Committees to finance part of their 
incremental operating costs for organizing Beneficiaries in their villages to carry 
out Project activities; and 

(iii) | Provision of Certification Incentive Payments to Beneficiaries to encourage them 

to go through the process necessary for their farms to be certified by the DOA as

pig manure.
