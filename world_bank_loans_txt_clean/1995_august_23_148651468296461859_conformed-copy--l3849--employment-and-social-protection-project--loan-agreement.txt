SCHEDULE 2 

Description of the Project 

The objectives. of the Project are to: (a) assist the 
Guarantor in choosing among options for structural reform in the 
telecommunications, electric power, secondary petrochemicals and 
transportation sectors; (b) develop a sound legal and regulatory 
framework, and establish and strengthen the administrative framework 
required therefor, in support of privatization initiatives in said 
sectors; (c) reduce, and if possible eliminate, constraints to 
privatization which may exist in said sectors; and (d) support the 
process of preparing privatizations in said sectors. 

The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such 
modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon 
from time to time to achieve such objectives: 

Part A: Cross-sectoral Assistance 

1. Provision to CID of legal, financial and economic technical 
assistance to develop approaches to privatization which are 
consistent across sectors, with a focus on: 

(a) generic issues relating to the mechanics of 
privatization (such as treatment of debt, environmental and other 
liabilities of privatizable enterprises; treatment of labor and tax 
matters; -and definition of contractual mechanisms to handle 
privatization transactions) ; 

(b) generic regulatory issues impinging on privatization 
(such as establishment of rules governing the award, amendment and 
revocation of concessions; definition and implementation of 
consistent competition-promotion policies; and preparation of 
principles to govern the design and establishment of sector 
regulatory institutions); and 

(c) generic issues relating to the encouragement of private 
sector participation in infrastructure sectors (such as development 
of financing vehicles for such participation and improvement of 
legal frameworks regarding matters such as security interests and 
alternative dispute resolution processes, but not including issues 
regarding the structure and operations of the Mexican judiciary and 
legal system in general). 

2. Carrying out of studies and training activities (in support of 
cross-sectoral initiatives developed pursuant to the technical 
assistance provided under Part A.1 of the Project). 

3. Provision of goods and equipment to CID (and Guarantor line 
ministries for the sectors covered by Part B.1 (a) of the Project) 
in support of their privatization and Project-execution functions.
Part B: Sector-specific Assistance 

1. (a) Provision of legal, financial and economic technical 
assistance to prepare for privatizations in the: (i) electric power 
sector; (ii) secondary petrochemicals sector; (iii) 
telecommunications sector (with respect to communications 
satellites, basic telephone services and radio spectrum use); and 
(iv) transportation sector (with respect to ports, railways and 
airports). 

(b) Provision of technical assistance to prepare for and 
proceed with regulatory initiatives related to the possible 
privatizations in the sectors referred to in Part B.1 (a) of the 
Project. 

2. Carrying out of studies and training activities (in support of 
sector-specific initiatives developed pursuant to the technical 
assistance provided under Part B.1 of the Project).
