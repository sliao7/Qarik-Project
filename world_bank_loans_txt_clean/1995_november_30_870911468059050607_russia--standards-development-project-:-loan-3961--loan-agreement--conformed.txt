SCHEDULE 2 

Description of the Project 

The objectives of the Project are to facilitate the Borrower's integration into 

international cooperative networks on product and quality standards, the harmonization 
of the Borrower's product standards with international standards and the upgrading of the 

certification capacity of Gost. 

The Project consists of the following paris, subject to such modifications thereof 
as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such 

objectives: 

Part A: 

Establishment of the Russian Data Center within Gost for the fulfillment of the 

task of the standards enquiry point, to act as electronic clearinghouse and depository for 

Russian and international standards required by the General Agreement on Tariffs and 

Trade, acquisition and installation of a large network and data base system needed 

therefor and carrying out of minor civil works required for the installation of large 

equipment. 

Part B: 

Identification of the Borrower's product standards within priority sectors, which 

do not conform with international standards, assessment of the extent of the divergence 
between nonconforming national and international standards, evaluation of the nature of 

constraints to harmonization of national and international standards, rewriting of 

nonconforming standards to render them compatible with international standards, and 

provision of consultants and translation services and acquisition and installation of 

standards data bases needed therefor. 

Part C: 

Strengthening of Gost's capacity to carry out testings and certifications of 

selected products through provision of training and testing, metrology and other 
equipment needed therefor with a view to gain international accreditation under the 

relevant systems.
