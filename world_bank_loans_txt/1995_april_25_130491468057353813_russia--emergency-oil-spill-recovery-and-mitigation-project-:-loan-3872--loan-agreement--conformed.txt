Description  of the Project

The objectives of the Project are to stabilize the oil spill area along the Kharyaga-
Usinsk  pipeline  prior  to  the  1995  spring  thaw  to  minimize  the  amount  of oil  released
during  runoff  and  prevent  ecological  damage  in the  Pechora  river  basin,  continue  the
clean  up  in  an  environmentally  appropriate  way and  minimize,  to  the  extent  possible,
damage  to  the  impacted  areas,  support  safe  pipeline  operations  in. the  near  term  and
evaluate  the need  for  a replacement  pipeline  investment  project  for the  longer term,  and
identify  and  implement  other  measures  to mitigate  against future  oil spills.

The Project consists of the following parts,  subject to such modifications  thereof
as  the  Borrower  and  the  Bank  may  agree  upon  from  time  to  time  to  achieve  such
objectives:
