SCHEDULE  2

Description  of  the  Project

The  objectives  of  the  Project  are:  (a)  to  assist  the  Guarantor
in  consolidating  the  transfer  of  management  and  operation  of
Irrigation  Districts  from  the  public  sector  to  Water  Users'
Organizations;  and  (b)  to  assist  Irrigation  Farmers  in  Participating
Irrigation  Districts  in  diversifying  their  agricultural  production
and  increasing  their  efficiency  in  the  use  of  land  and  water
resources.
