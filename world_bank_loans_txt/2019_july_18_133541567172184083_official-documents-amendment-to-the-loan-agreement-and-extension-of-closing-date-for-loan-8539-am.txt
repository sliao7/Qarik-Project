We also refer to your letter dated May 29, 2019, requesting a restructuring of the Project which requires certain amendments to the Loan Agreement. Please note that unless the context otherwise requires, the capitalized terms used in this Agreement has the meanings ascribed to them in the General Conditions or in the Loan Agreement. In light of the above, we propose to amend the Loan Agreement as follows: 1. All references to the term “Project Implementation Department” is replaced with the term “Office of the Prime Minister” in the entire Loan Agreement. 2. The acronym “PID” is replaced with the acronym “OPM” in the entire Loan Agreement. . All references to the term “Civil Service Council” is replaced with the term “Civil Service Office” in the entire Loan Agreement. 4. The acronym “CSC” is replaced with the acronym “CSO” in the entire Loan Agreement. 5. Section I. A.1(a) and (c) of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement are hereby amended to read as follows: ‘(a) for the purpose of ensuring the proper oversight, coordination and execution of the Project, vest the overall responsibility for implementation of the Project in the PSRC which shall: (i) act as the Project Steering Committee; (ii) be headed by the Borrower’s Deputy Prime Minister or Chief of Staff, who is also the Project Director; and (111) be responsible for providing strategic guidance for the Project and approving the Project Operational Manual, Project annual budgets, and key financial management and procurement documents.” ‘“(c) for the purpose of complying with the fiduciary aspects of the Project, vest in OPM the responsibilities of financial management and procurement, and ensure that OPM, including PMG, has adequate number of staff with terms of reference and qualifications satisfactory to the Bank to fulfill its responsibilities with respect to Project implementation.”Section IV. B.2 of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as follows: “9D.
