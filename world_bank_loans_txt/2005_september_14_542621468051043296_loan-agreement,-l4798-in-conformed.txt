SCHEDULE 2 

Description of the Project 

The objective of the Project is to improve urban infrastructure services in Tamil 
Nadu  in  a  sustainable  manner  through:    (a)  strengthening  the  managerial,  financial  and 
technical capabilities of ULBs; (b) mobilizing resources and securing sustainable funding 
sources for urban infrastructure investment by TNUDF; and (c) providing incentives for 
investments in low-income neighborhoods through the use of Capital Grants.

as  the  Borrower  and  the  Bank  may  agree  upon  from  time  to  time  to  achieve  such 
objectives:
