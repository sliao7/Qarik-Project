The  objectives  of the Project  are:  (a) to increase  highway  capacity  in the  Shanghai-
Hangzhou  corridor  with  a  view  to  relieving  congestion  and  promoting  economic
development;  (b) to improve  road safety  in Northern Zhejiang  through the implementation
of a pilot  road safety  program;  and  (c) to develop  highway  sector institutional  capacity  in
Zhejiang and Shanghai.

The  Project  consists of the  following  parts,  subject to  such  modifications thereof as

the Borrower and the Bank  may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives:
