The Project is part of the Power Sector Investment Program and consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives: Part A: Policy Studies and Technical Assistance   1, Energy Sector Restructuring   Provision of technical assistance for the: (a) drafting of regulations to the Electricity Law, the CNE Law, and the CREH Law; and (b) implementation and operation of CREH and CNE including the provision of equipment required therefor and training of the personnel of CREH and CNE. 2. Power Sector Strengthening   Provision of technical assistance for the promotion of: (a) private sector participation in power generation; and (b) the restructuring of CEL in accordance with the Electricity Law, CNE Law and CREH Law. 3. CEL's Studies Provision or technical assistance for: (a) the preparation of feasibility studies for the El Tigre and El Zapotillo hydroelectric projects or such other project as the Bank may agree based on the conclusions of the prefeasibility studies currently being carried out by CEL;    -19- (b) the design and supervision of the rehabilitation and expansion of the hydroelectric plants referred to in Part B of the Project; (c) the preparation of a study on the environmental impact of existing power stations; . (d) the improvement of CEL's information and telecommunications systems; and (e) the training of CEL's staff in technical, financial and managerial matters. Part B: Rehabilitation and Expansion of CEL’s Hydroelectric Power Plants   1. Rehabilitation, including installation of the electromechanical equipment required therefor, of CEL's following plants: "5 de Noviembre", "Cerrén Grande", and "Guajoyo".   2. Installation of a new power generating unit at the "Cerron Grande" plant. ° 3. Installation of equipment for sedimentation control at the hydroelectric stations referred to in Parts B.1 and B.2 above. Part C: Modernization of CEL's Information and Telecommunications Systems   Modernization of CEL's information and telecommunications systems, including its central load dispatch center, through the provision of the equipment and spare parts required therefor. The Project is expected to be completed by December 31, 1999.      -20 -     SCHEDULE 3 Amortization Schedule Payment of Principal (Expressed in Date Payment Due Dollars)* October 15, 2000 1,260,000 April 15, 2001 1,305,000 October 15, 2001 1,350,000 April 15, 2002 1,400,000 October 15, 2002 1,445,000 April 15, 2003 1,495,000 October 15, 2003 1,550,000 April 15, 2004 1,605,000 October 15, 2004 1,660,000 April 15, 2005 1,715,000 October 15, 2005 1,775,000 April 15, 2006 1,840,000 October 15, 2006 1,905,000 April 15, 2007 1,970,000 October 15, 2007 2,040,000 April 15, 2008 2,110,000 October 15, 2008 2,185,000 April 15, 2009 2,260,000 October 15, 2009 2,340,000 April 15, 2010 2,420,000 October 15, 2010 2,505,000 April 15, 2011 2,590,000 October 15, 2011 2,685,000 April 15, 2012 2,775,000 October 15, 2012 2,875,000 April 15, 2013 2,975,000 October 15, 2013 3,075,000 April 15, 2014 3,185,000 October 15, 2014 3,295,000 April 15, 2015 3,410,000. * The figures in this column represent the amount in Dollars to be repaid, except as provided in Section 4.04 (d) of the General Conditions.      _-21- SCHEDULE 4 Procurement and Consultants' Services Section I.
