SCHEDULE 2

Description of the Project

The objective of the Project is to assist in financing pollution abatement 

investments as will contribute to the reduction of industrial pollution.

The Project consists of the following, subject to such modifications thereof as 

the Bank and the Borrower may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objective:

The financing, through the provision of Sub-loans by the Apex Bank and the 

Participating Banks, of specific cost-effective pollution abatement investments by 
Beneficiaries to bring effluent discharges and air emissions of said Beneficiaries’ 
existing facilities towards compliance with the applicable laws and regulations of the 
Guarantor.

The Project is expected to be completed by March 31, 2003.
