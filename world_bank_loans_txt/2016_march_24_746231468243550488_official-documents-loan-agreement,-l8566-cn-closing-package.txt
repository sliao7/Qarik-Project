SCHEDULE1

Project Description

The  objective of the Project  is  to  improve the  efficiency  and  environmental  performance
of  heating  systems  in  selected  areas  within  participating  municipalities/counties  of  Hebei
Province.

The Project consists of the following parts:

Part 1: District Heating Subprojects

1.1 

1.2 

1.3 

1.4 

Chengde  District  Heating  and  Energy  Efficiency  Subproject.  Rehabilitation  and
expansion  of district  heating  facilities  in  Chengde's  Shuangqio  district,  including:  (a)
extension  of district  heating  primary  network,  (b)  renovation  of district heating  primary
and secondary network,  and (c)  construction of substations.

Zhangiiakou  District  Heating  Subproiect.  Rehabilitation  and  expansion  of  district
heating  facilities  in Zhangjiakou's  Qiaodong  district,  including:  (a) extension.of district
heating  primary  network;  (b)  construction  of substations;  (c)  conversion  of coal-fired
boilers  to  natural  gas  boilers;  (d)  construction  of  a  pressure  isolation  station;  and  (e)
installation  of heat  meters  and  a  supervisory  control  and  data  acquisition  system  for
district heating.

Pingshan  District- Heating  and  Energy  Efficiency  Subproject.  Rehabilitation  and
expansion  of district  heating  facilities  in  Pingshan  County,  including:  (a)  extension  of
district  heating primary  network;  (b)  renovation  of district  heating  primary  network;  (c)
construction  of  substations,  and  rehabilitation/expansion  of  existing  substations;  (d)
installation  of  heat  meters;  (e)  installation  of  automatic  control  systems  for  existing
substations;  (f)  rehabilitation  of  water  treatment  stations;  and  (g)  installation  of  a
supervisory  control  and  data  acquisition  system  and  a  central  monitoring  and  control
system for district heating.

Xingtai  Industrial  Waste  Heat  District  Heating  Subproiect.  Construction  of  district
heating  facilities  in  Xingtai  Municipality,  including:  (a)  construction  of an  industrial
waste  heat  water  network  and  extension  of  primary  district  heating  network-  (b)
construction  of a  primary  station  and  substations;  and  (c)  installation  of a  supervisory
control  and data acquisition system  for district  heating.

Part 2: Institutional Capacity Building  and Project Management  Support

Provision  of:  (a)  Project  management  support;  (b)  capacity  building activities  related to
district  heating  management,  operation  and  maintenance,  and  Project  management;  and
(c) training  activities,  technical  assistance  support,  and knowledge  sharing  activities;  all
to the Hebei Project  Management  Office  and the Project  Companies.
