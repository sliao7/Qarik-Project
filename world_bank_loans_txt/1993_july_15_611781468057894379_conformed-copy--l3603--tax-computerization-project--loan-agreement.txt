SCHEDULE 2

Description of the Project

The objective of the Project is to assist the Borrower in establishing and 

thereafter operating a modern computerized integrated tax administration system 
throughout its territory.

The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications 

thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon
from time to time to achieve such objectives:

Part A:

BIR Computerized Tax Administration System

1.
Establishing a computerized integrated tax administration system to process and 
audit all tax returns, including personal individual income tax, corporate income tax, 
excise tax, other percentage taxes, withholding tax and value added tax.

Provision of computer hardware and software, and related data-entry processing 
2.
equipment and materials necessary for establishing the tax administration system and 
collecting the taxes referred to in Part A.1 above, comprising, inter alia: (a) a 
mainframe computer at BIR’s national office; (b) communication and microcomputer 
systems and data©entry and computing facilities at about nineteen (19) BIR regional 
offices and at about forty-four (44) BIR revenue district offices; and (c) compatible 
computer software in respect of the foregoing.

3.
vehicles necessary for the operation and management of BIR.

Provision of priority office equipment and related materials and utility 

Part B:

BOC Computerized Tax Administration System

Establishing a computerized integrated tax administration system to process 

1.
customs taxes and other customs related transactions and improve the overall 
processing efficiency in respect of the import and export of goods within the 
Borrower's territory.

Provision of computer hardware and system software, and related data-entry 

2.
processing equipment and material, for establishing the tax administration system and 
collecting the taxes referred to in Part B.1 above, comprising, inter alia: (a) two 
(2) separate computers for software development and integrating all tax data at the 
Office of the Commissioner of BOC, and providing related data support and valuation 
systems; (b) microcomputer systems for the Borrower's: Manila Port, Food Terminal Inc. 
of the Manila Port, Manila International Container Port, Batangas Port, Cebu Port, 
Manila International Airport and Cebu International Airport; (c) microcomputer systems 
for integrated processing of export-related taxes; and (d) compatible computer 
software in respect of the foregoing.

3.
Provision of a customs application software system for, customs tax 
administration, including provision of related technical training for operating, 
maintaining and customization and developing system.

Institutional Strenghtening
