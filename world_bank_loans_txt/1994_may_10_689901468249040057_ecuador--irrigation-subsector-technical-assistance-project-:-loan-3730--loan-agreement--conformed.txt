The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives. Part A: Institutional Reorganization and Strengthening Development of the regulatory and administrative framework required to rationalize the Borrower’s role in its irrigation subsector, while simultaneously reorganizing and strengthening the corresponding Borrower’s institutions, through: (a) organization of CNRH; (b) support of the decentralization process by strengthening the Regional Agencies and adopting a new organizational structure of INERHI; and (c) preparation of the administrative framework required for the operation of the decentralized structure referred to in (b) above, involving procedures and mechanisms to implement new strategies and policies with relation to: (i) water resource management; (ii) water rights and concessions; (iii) water charges and cost recovery mechanisms for & rehabilitation, operation and maintenance of irrigation schemes included in the Pilot Project; and          ~ 21 - (iv) participation of the private sector in the identification, design, construction, operation and maintenance of irrigation schemes. Part B: Private Sector Development Program Development of suitable and sustainable mechanisms for the Regional Agencies to promote user-financed design, construction, rehabilitation, completion and operation and maintenance of irrigation schemes, and to foster the transfer of such responsibilities to private users, through: (a) carrying out of Pilot Projects; (b) strengthening of the administration and productive capacity of water users’ organizations interested in the carrying out of Pilot Projects; (c) carrying out of a comprehensive training and communications program (aimed at disseminating the experience obtained in the Pilot Projects) for the benefit of water users; and (d) carrying out of a comprehensive review of the current status of privately-developed irrigation schemes.
