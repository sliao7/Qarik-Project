Borrower’s Ministry of Labor prior to labor force reduction motivated by economic 
reasons; and

a list of products which will no longer be subject to quantitative 

restrictions and non-tariff barriers as of January 1, 1992, and an indicative list to 
be finalized through the issue of the decree referred to under paragraph 2 (c) of
