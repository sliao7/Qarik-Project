The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives: Part A: Contracting of MSP’s provision of health services l. Carry out a review of the national and international experiences with respect to the decentralization and contracting of the provision of health services. 2. Establish and analyze the different alternatives to contract the provision of health services of MSP with Eligible Entities, including, inter alia, the design of: (a) an operational framework for each alternative; and (b) the mechanism for the payment of the health services to be provided to the beneficiaries by the Eligible Entities as a result of the contracting of MSP’s provision of health services. 3. Carry out a pilot program consisting, inter alia, of: (a) selecting the Eligible Departments; (b) selecting the Eligible Entities; (c) drafting the legal documents for each alternative referred to in Part A.2 above; (d) contracting the provision of health services of MSP with Eligible Entities within Eligible Departments in the Borrower’s territory as a result of the activity to be carried out under Part A.2 above; and (e) implementing the provision of health services to be provided by the Eligible Entities. 4. Strengthen the management of Eligible Entities (including the acquisition and utilization of equipment and provision of training required therefor) which would carry out the provision of health services under the pilot program referred to in Part A.3 above. 5. Design and implement a system to evaluate the pilot program referred to in Part A.3 above.        - 18- Part B: Institutional Development 1. Carry out a review of the public health regulatory framework, and improve, if needed, such regulatory framework in order to implement: (i) the contracting of MSP’s provision of health services with Eligible Entities under the pilot program referred to in Part A.3 above; and (ii) the institutional reorganization of the public health sector to be carried out under Part B.2 (b) below. 2. (a) Design the institutional reorganization of the public health sector in order to strengthen MSP’s role to regulate and finance the provision of health services; and (b) implement the institutional reorganization referred to in (a) herein. 3. (a) Design of a public health’s sector management information system; and (b) implement the system referred to in (a) herein within the Eligible Departments. 4, Strengthen the technical and management capacity of health sector professionals within the Borrower’s territory through the provision of scholarships to Eligible Professionals. Part C: Health Service 1. Carry out a priority health program consisting, inter alia, of: (a) a review of the international experiences in the definition of disease priorities and the establishment of cost-effective public health programs; (b) an analysis of the experiences mentioned in (a) herein which are applicable to Uruguay; (c) a review of the current standards for the provision of health services, and improve, if needed, such standards; (d) the application of global burden of disease and cost-effective methodologies to Eligible Entities within Eligible Departments, and the dissemination of the application of such methodologies to other departments (Departamentos) within the Borrower’s territory; (e) the carrying out of Eligible Public Health Programs resulting from the review referred to in (a) herein, including, if required, the strengthening of such programs; and (f) the evaluation of the impact of rationalizing the disease priorities.        - 19 - 2.
