The Project consists of the following parts: - Part A: Mengzi City 1, Optimization of the functional hierarchy and overall efficiency of Mengzi City’s road network through, inter alia, the construction of selected. missing links in the road network and rehabilitation of selected road corridors, including associated facilities. Implementation of integrated corridor management improvements on selected corridors in Mengzi City, incorporating, inter alia, civil works, intelligent transport system and road safety design, education and traffic regulation, parking management, and e-bike management. Improvement of public transport services through, inter alia: (a) construction of bus depots and terminals; (b) construction of bus stops and bus bays; and (c) development and implementation of a bus-location and monitoring system, and a real-time passenger information system. ~ . vs we at * Development and implementation of safe school transportation demonstration programs, ‘including through: (a) development of a model school transport zone; (b) piloting of Part B: home-to-school transportation demonstrations; and (c) carrying out of public campaigns, trainings, and provision of technical assistance. Enhancement of the institutional capacity of Mengzi Municipality, including through: (a) carrying out of urban transport related studies; (b) provision of project management and technical support; (c) carrying out of training and knowledge-sharing activities with a focus on urban transport and road safety initiatives; and (d) carrying out of public communication and education campaigns. Jianshui County : Implementation of integrated corridor management improvements on selected corridors in Jianshui County, incorporating, inter alia, civil works, intelligent transport system and road safety design, education and traffic regulation, parking management, and e-bike management. Improvement of public transport services through, inter alia: (a) construction of bus interchanges and depots; and (b) development of a bus-location and monitoring system,. and a real-time passenger information system. ~5-              Construction of the South Bypass, including associated facilities, aimed at alleviating through traffic in Jianshui County’s downtown area. Enhancement of the institutional capacity. of Jianshui County, including through: (a) carrying out of urban transport related studies; (b) provision of project management and technical support; (c) carrying out of training and knowledge-sharing activities with a focus on urban transport and road safety initiatives; and (d) carrying out of public communication and education campaigns.SCHEDULE 2 Project Execution Section I.
