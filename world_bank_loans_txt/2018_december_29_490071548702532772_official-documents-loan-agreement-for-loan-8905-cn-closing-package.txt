SCHEDULE 1 

Program Description 

The objective of the  Program is  to  improve  the  rural  road network connectivity,  safety, 

quality, and its management in the Participating Counties of Anhui Province. 

The Program consists of the following activities: 

Smooth Traffic Program and Natural Village Road Program 

Upgrading,  reconstruction  and  pavement  improvement, of selected  county and  township 
roads. 

Widening and/or paving of selected village roads. 

Safe Road Program 

Improvement of rural  road signs,  markings,  installation of guardrail  and roadside hazard 
management, as well as other safety facilities. 

C. 

Damaged Bridge Improvement Program 

Improvement and/or reparation of rural bridge assets, including dual carriageway bridges. 

Road Maintenance Program 

Maintenance of rural roads including routine, minor and periodic maintenance, as well as 
rehabilitation and improvement. 

Institutional Capacity Building Program 

Supporting  the  reform  of  county-level  transport  bureaus  and  related  government 
institutions involved in road management at the municipal and county levels. 

Provision of technical assistance and training on Program-related activities including, inter 
alia, on asset management, carrying out of impact analysis' activities, and monitoring and 
evaluation.

"Three Excellence Criteria" means all three of the following Program Implementing Entity 
modules: 

(a)  Excellence in construction:  At  least four of the five  following  objectives are met 
on  a  regular  basis:  (i)  80  percent  of the  planned  construction  task  for  the  year  is 
executed;  (ii)  appropriate  technical  standards  are  applied  for  county  and  township 
roads; (iii) procedures from the initial design to the completion of the works are applied 
in  a  satisfactory  manner;  (iv)  the  engineering  addresses  quality  and  safety  in  the 
construction;  and  (v)  ancillary  facilities  such  as  drainage  systems,  bus  stops  and 
parking are built along with the main infrastructure. 

(b)  Excellence  in  management:  (i)  The  county  and  township-level  rural  road 
management organizations should be operational and capable of performing standard 
management  operations;  (ii)  recurrent  costs  for  those  organizations  are  part  of 
the budget of the county administrations; (iii) management of rural roads is part of the 
evaluation  of  county  governments;  and  (iv)  expenditures  related  to  rural  roads 
are disclosed. 

(c)  Excellence  in  maintenance:  (i)  routine  maintenance  activities  for  county  and 
township levels should be fully funded and taking place on, at least, 70 percent of the 
respective  networks;  (ii)  promotion  of market-based  maintenance;  and  (iii)  periodic 
maintenance and rehabilitation should be fully  funded and suffice to  repair at  least 4 
percent of the county and township roads annually.
