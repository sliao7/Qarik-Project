SCHEDULE 2

Description of the Project

The objective of the Project is to assist the Borrower in improving the quality 
and coverage of junior secondary education in its provinces of East Java and East Nusa 
Tenggara, through the expansion of access to junior secondary education in a 
cost-effective and equitable way, the improvement of teaching at junior secondary 
schools, and the strengthening of the management capacity at all levels of the 
education system.

The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications 

thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such 
objectives:

PART A:
 
1.
 

Increasing Access to Junior Secondary Education

Increasing the access of students to junior secondary education through:

(a)

construction in remote areas of East Java of six-classroom and 

three-classroom schools, and teacher dormitories at new three-classroom schools and at 
existing schools, and provision of related furniture; and

(b)

construction in remote areas of East Nusa Tenggara of six-classroom and 

three-classroom schools, additional classrooms in existing schools, and teacher 
dormitories at new three-classroom schools, and provision of related furniture.

2.
to SMP Terbuka learning centers, and training of supervising tutors.

Tenggara.
