SCHEDULE 2

Description of the Project

The objectives of the Project are to (a) alleviate an acute shortage of peaking 
power and enable more efficient use of power plants, (b) improve the quality of power 
supply and enhancing socio-economic conditions in densely populated East China 
regions, (c) improve load management and promote energy conservation, (d) support 
institutional development of ECEPUC, (e) contribute to the
development of an improved power supply utility regulatory framework, and (f) assist 
in transferring new power technologies and in applying modern power system operation 
optimization methods.
