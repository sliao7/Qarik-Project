Part 1: Part 2: Part 3: The Project consists of the following parts: Road Rehabilitation Rehabilitating approximately 108 kilometers of the Kyiv-Kharkiv-Dovzhansky road in the Poltava Oblast, including civil works for the rehabilitation and strengthening of the existing road, widening of certain sections from two lanes to four lanes, and building three bypasses around populated areas. Road Safety Improvements Improving road safety along various road corridors or sections in Ukraine. Institutional Support and Strengthening Provision of technical advisory services, Training and equipment to enhance road management within the territory of the Borrower according to international practice, including: (i) in the areas of road safety, an assessment of approximately 12,000-15,000 kilometers of main roads, including the coding of data based on the iRAP methodology, data aialysis and road safety “star rating” of road sections, identification of the most suitable and appropriate road safety measures and interventions for each road, and detailed design of road safety measures for the high-priority sections of corridors; and (ii) the support for the development of a Strategic Investment Program for Road Sector Development until 2030.SCHEDULE 2 Project Execution Section I.
