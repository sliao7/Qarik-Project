SCHEDULE 2 Description of the Project The Project’s general objective is to support the Provinces in undertaking public sector reform programs consistent with the Borrower’s overall public sector reform. The specific objectives of the Project are: (a) to assist the Provinces in implementing appropriate financial management reforms to generate current account surpluses and to strengthen their capacity to plan, program, finance, execute, and monitor investment programs that are economically efficient, financially sustainable, institutionally manageable, and environmentally sound; and (b) to provide financing for institutional development and physical investments that will promote provincial economic development. The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives: Part A: Public Investment Subprojects Carrying out of projects aimed at improving public services and infrastructure through physical investments that are part of approved FAIPs, with emphasis on maintenance programs, rehabilitation of existing works and completion of unfinished works, encompassing public infrastructure and facilities (such as roads, drainage, wa.-r supply and sanitation, schools, and health posts) not eligible for financing under other Bank-financed projects. Part B: Institutional Development Subprojects Carrying out of projects at both the national and provincial levels of government for institutional development consistent with the objectives of the Project, including, inter alia, improvement of revenue administration systems, cadastres, integrated project evaluation, and financial management and control (including improvement of the coverage of the stock of provincial debt in the provincial financial management information system), training of professional staff, and analysis of the desirability and feasibility of the privatization of Provincial Entities. * OK The Project is expected to be completed by December 31, 2001.          -20- SCHEDULE 3 Amortization Schedule Payment of Principa! The Subsidiary Loan Agreements shall include terms and conditions satisfactory to the Bank, including: (1) The Subsidiary Loan shall be used for the financing of Eligible Subprojects and shall be repaid within a period of up to 15 years, including up to 5 years of grace. (2) The Subsidiary Loans shall be denominated in dollar equivalents and the amounts withdrawn thereunder and outstanding (together with interest and other charges thereon) shall be payable in the same currency in which the principal amount of the Loan and interest and other charges thereon are to be paid by the Borrower to the Bank pursuant to Article IV of the General Conditions. (3) The Subsidiary Loans shall carry the same interest rate applicable to the Loan pursuant to Section 2.05 of this Agreement. (4) The Borrower shall allocate the commitment charge applicable to the Loan pursuant to Section 2.04 of this Agreement proportionately among the Participating Provinces according to criteria satisfactory to the Bank. (5) As part of the Subsidiary Loan, an amount of up to 2% of the amount of the Subsidiary Loan disbursed to finance Eligible Subprojects shall be debited to the Participating Province for the purposes of covering the costs of the CEU. (6) The full amount of the Initial Allocation shall be available for each Participating Province for a period of one year from the date of this Agreement, and any amounts not committed in terms satisfactory to the Borrower and the Bank after such period shall be made available to the Participating Provinces willing to continue participating in the execution of the Project in amounts and terms specified in the Manual, provided, however, that the failure of a Participating Province to carry out a FAIP in the terms approved by the Bank or any default under a Subsidiary Loan Agreement shall result in the cancellation of any and all amounts of the Participating Province’s Initial Allocation up to then not disbursed or committed under the Subsidiary Loan Agreement, as provided in the Manual. The Borrower, through the CEU, shall promptly inform the Bank and the respective Participating Province of any such cancellation.      - 29 - (7) All amounts of the Loan not included in the Initial Allocation of the Provinces or which are taken out of the Initial Allocation of the Provinces pursuant to the provisions of this Agreement or the Manual, shall be available to the Participating Provinces willing to continue participating in the execution of the Project. (8) The Participating Province shall procure goods, works and consultants’ services, and shall maintain records and separate accounts in respect of its activities under the Project, all as provided in the Loan Agreement. (9) The Participating Province shall carry out the obligations set forth in Sections 9.04, 9.05, 9.06, 9.07, 9.08, and 9.09 of the General Conditions (relating to insurance, use of goods and services, plans and schedules, records and reports, maintenance and land acquisition, respectively) in respect of its activities under the Project.
