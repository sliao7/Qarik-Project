The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objective: Part A: Carrying out of a program to rehabilitate and modernize administrative services, through the provision of office equipment, office technology, information systems, and related and necessary training and services. Part B: Development and implementation of a program to: (1) establish a modern database for the civil service based on a comprehensive census of all civil servants; (2) design and initiate a long-term strategy for comprehensive public administration and civil service reform through: (i) the carrying out of reviews of, inter alia, civil service regulations, salary levels and structure, mandates and missions of ministries and core agencies, and control and audit procedures; and (11) the organization of workshops for public and private sector participants, including those involved in the implementation of Part B (2) (i) above, to facilitate dissemination of information and consensus building for long-term administrative reforms. kkekK The Project is expected to be completed by December 31, 1998.      -13- SCHEDULE 3 Amortization Schedule Payment of Principal Date Payment Due (expressed in dollars)* On each April 1 and October 1 835,000.00 beginning April 1, 2001 through April 1, 2012 And on October 1, 2012 795,000.00     * The figures in this column represent dollar equivalents determined as of the respective dates of withdrawal.
