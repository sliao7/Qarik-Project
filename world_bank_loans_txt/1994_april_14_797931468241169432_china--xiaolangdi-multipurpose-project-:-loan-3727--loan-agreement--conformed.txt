The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives. Part A: Xiaolangdi Dam 1. Construction and equipping of an earth and rockfill dam (with a sloping core) with a maximum height of 154 meters, a crest length of 1,370 meters and a volume of about 47 million cubic meters on the Yellow River at Xiaolangdi in Henan Province, about 130 kilometers downstream from the Sanmenxia Dam. 2. Construction and equipping of a common intake structure consis- ting of nine large intake towers feeding sixteen large diameter tunnels and a surface spillway and emergency spillway (for river diversion, flood handling, sediment management and power releases), discharging into a common plunge pool. Part Bs Power Supply Construction and equipping of an underground powerhouse, housing six 300 megawatt power generating units with associated switchyards and transformers. Part C: Environment Carrying out an environmental management plan in connection with the construction and operation of the Xiaolangdi dam.        -17-   Part D: Institutional Support 1. Strengthening YRWHDC’s management capability to implement the Project and operate the Project facilities through provision of training and consultants’ services in areas including design and construction management, contract management, project scheduling, cost control, claims management, financial management, management information systems, organizational support, corporate planning and personnel management. 2. Strengthening the management capability of MWR and YRCC to support the reform process in the water sector through provision of training and consultants’ services and undertaking studies in: international accounting procedures; sustainable resource mobiliza- tion for water resources projects; water pricing; water licensing; effective river basin organization; and water dispatching systems for basins. The Project is expected to be completed by December 31, 2001.        - 18 - SCHEDULE 3 Amortization Schedule Payment of Principal Date Payment Due (expressed in dollars)* On each January 1 and July 1 beginning January 1, 2002   through January 1, 2014 17,690,000 and on July 1, 2014 17,750,000 * The figures in this column represent dollar equivalents determined as of the respective dates of withdrawal.
