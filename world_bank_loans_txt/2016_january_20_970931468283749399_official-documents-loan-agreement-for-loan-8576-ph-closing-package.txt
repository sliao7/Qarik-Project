Actions under the Program Actions Taken Under the Program. The actions taken by the Borrower under the Program to enhance its technical and financial capacity to reduce disaster risk and manage the socio-economic and fiscal impacts of natural disasters include the following: 1. The National Economic and Development Authority has issued the midterm update to the Philippine Development Plan, revising the Borrower’s investment programming strategy to prioritize disaster risk reduction and management according to levels of vulnerability to multiple hazards. 2. The Borrower has provided specific allocations for risk resiliency programs, through the 2015 General Appropriations Act. 3. The Borrower’s Department of Finance has adopted the Disaster Risk Financing and Insurance Strategy, which provides a comprehensive framework for the implementation: of financial protection solutions against natural disasters at national, local and individual level. 4. The Borrower has included special provisions in the 2015 General Appropriations Act to allow four agencies (Department of Education, Department of Public Works and Highways, Department of Health and Department of Transportation and Communications) to use the National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Fund to pay for insurance premiums.-7- Section II.
