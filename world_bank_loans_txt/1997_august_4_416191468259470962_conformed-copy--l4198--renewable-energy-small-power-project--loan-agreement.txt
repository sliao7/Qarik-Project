SCHEDULE 2

Description of the Project

The objectives of the Project are to assist the Borrower to: (a) facilitate 

participation by the private sector in the development of small renewable energy 
projects in Indonesia on a commercially sustainable basis; (b) promote environmentally 
sound energy resource development and reduce the dependence of the Borrower’s energy 
sector on fossil fuels; (c) bring about cost effective mitigation of carbon dioxide 
(CO2) emissions; and (d) strengthen the Borrower’s institutional capacity to support 
and sustain renewable energy development.

thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such 
objectives:
