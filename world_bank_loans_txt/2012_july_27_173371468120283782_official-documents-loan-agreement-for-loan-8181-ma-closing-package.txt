SCHEDULE  1

Program Description

The  objective  of  the  Program  is  to  improve  access  to  and/or  use  of  enhanced
participatory  local  governance  mechanisms,  basic  infrastructure,  social  services  and
economic  opportunities  in the Program  Area.

The  Program  consists  of the  following  activities to  be  implemented  from January
1, 2012  to  December  31,  2015  in  the  Program  Area  under  the  rural  sub-program,  the
urban  sub-program  and the  cross-cutting  sub-program  of the  INDH  2  Program:

Part 1:  Rural  Sub-Program

Carrying  out  Projects  identified  and  prioritized  through  a  participatory  planning
process,  in  selected  rural  communes,  aimed  at:  (i)  improving  living  conditions  of the
population:  (ii)  supporting  access  to  basic  infrastructure  and  services;  (iii)  promoting
income  generating  activities;  (iv)  promoting  social,  cultural  and  sporting  activities;  and
(v)  strengthening capacities.

Part 2:  Urban  Sub-Program

Carrying  out  Projects  identified  and  prioritized  through  a  participatory  planning
process,  in  selected  urban  neighborhoods,  aimed  at  (i) improving  living  conditions  of the
population,  (ii)  supporting  access  to  basic  infrastructure  and  services;  (iii)  promoting
income  generating  activities;  (iv)  promoting  social,  cultural  and  sporting  activities;  and
(v)  strengthening capacities.

Part 3:  Cross-Cutting  Sub-Program

1. 

2. 

Carrying  out  Projects  identified  through  a  competitive  selection  process,  in non-
targeted  areas  under  other  sub-programs,  aimed  at  (i)  promoting  economic
inclusion  of poor  and  vulnerable  persons;  (ii)  improving  living  conditions  of the
population;  (iii)  supporting  access  to  basic  infrastructure  and  services;  (iv)
promoting  income  generating  activities;  (v)  promoting  social,  cultural  and
sporting  activities;  and  (vi) strengthening  capacities.

Supporting  communication,  monitoring  and  evaluation,  and  capacity  building
activities  at  the  local  level  and  providing  technical  assistance  to  Partners  and
other  relevant entities.
