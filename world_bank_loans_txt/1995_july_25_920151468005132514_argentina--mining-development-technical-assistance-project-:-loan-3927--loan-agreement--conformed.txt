The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives: Part A: Policy Development 1. Legal Framework Development (a) Drafting of modern mining legislation and regulations for the Borrower and the Participating Provinces. (b) Preparation of uniform procedures for administration of mining rights, including award of exploration permits and mining concessions. (c) Drafting of training manuals for officials of the Participating Provinces dealing with mining legislation, regulations and procedures. 2. SINATEM Studies Carrying out of studies aimed at: (a) determining the supply and demand for the existing mining research and development services in the territory of the Borrower; and (b) analyzing the present and potential roles of small and medium mining in the territory of the Borrower, identifying the respective issues and proposing solutions.            -21- Part B: Policy Implementation 1. Institutional Capacity Development (a) Carrying out of an institutional framework development program comprising: (i) (ii) the definition of an institutional model for the mining sector of the Borrower (including the geological service) and the Participating Provinces; and the upgrading of the facilities of SM’s central offices and selected regional offices, DNM, DNSG, DPMs and other agencies of the Participating Provinces. (b) Carrying out of a training program for officials of the Borrower and the Participating Provinces active in the mining sector and environmental matters, aimed at: (i) (ii) (iii) enhancing their managerial and technical skills; building their capacity on taxation, accounting and financial issues relevant for the enforcement of the Mining Investment Law and other fiscal provisions affecting mining activities; and assisting agencies of the Borrower and the Participating Provinces involved in SINATEM: (A) to adjust their roles to the new institutional policies; and (B) to participate in the implementation of other parts of the Project. 2. Cadastré and Registry System Transformation of the Participating Provinces’ existing physical registries into modern mining cadastres. 3.
