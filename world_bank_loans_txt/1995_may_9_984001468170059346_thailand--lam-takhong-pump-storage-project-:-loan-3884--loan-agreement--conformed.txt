The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives: 1. Construction of an underground power house for four (4) reversipdle Francis-type generation units, each with an output of 250 MW at a rated net head of 357 m, for a total capacity of about one thousand (1000) megawatt ("MW"). 2. Construction of an upper water reservoir on a nearby plateau with a rectangular pond with a surface area of 0.34 sq km and a volume of 9.9 million cubic meters, bounded by a rockfill embankment (with a maximum height of 50 m and a volume of 5.4 million cubic meters) and lined with an asphaltic membrane. 3. Construction of two (2) inlet tunnels connecting the upper reservoir to the power house. 4. Construction of two (2) steel-lined penstock tunnels of 6 m diameter and 651 m length, connecting the intake structure to the power house. 5. Construction of about 2 x 230 kv double circuit power transmission lines totalling a length of fifteen kilometers (15. kms). 6. Construction of a switchyard and control buildings. 7. Provision of necessary goods, equipment and materials necessary in respect of the foregoing. 8. Provision of technical assistance services to: (a) strengthen the Borrower’s capabilities for environmental management; and .        - 21 - : (b) assist in carrying out a study on the rationalization of bulk supply tariffs.
