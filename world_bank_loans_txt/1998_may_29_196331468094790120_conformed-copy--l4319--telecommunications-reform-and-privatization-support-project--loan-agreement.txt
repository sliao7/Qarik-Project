SCHEDULE 2

Description of the Project

The objectives of the Project are to: (i) assist Romania in reforming the 

telecommunications sector and privatizing existing public sector telecommunications 
facilities; (ii) provide the infrastructure to enable the Borrower to maximize the 
productive use of the radio frequency spectrum and to improve frequency assignment 
criteria for the further expansion of wireless communications services provision by 
the private sector; and (iii) support the Borrower in performing regulatory functions 
over all networks and services in the telecommunications sector.

The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications 

thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such 
objectives:

Part A:

Telecommunications Sector Reform

(1)   Development of a policy, legal and regulatory framework for the 
telecommunications sector through the provision of technical assistance and training.

(2)

Privatization of Rom Telecom through the provision of technical assistance and 

training.  

Part B:

Regulatory Agency Development

(1)
regulatory agency for the telecommunications sector.  

Expansion of the regulatory competencies of the Borrower as the independent

Project implementation support for the Borrower through the provision of 

(3)

development program, and market-based licensing, and training in spectrum management 
and related services.
