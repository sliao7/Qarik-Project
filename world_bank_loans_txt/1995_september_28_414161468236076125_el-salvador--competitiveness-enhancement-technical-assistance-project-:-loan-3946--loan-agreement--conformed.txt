SCHEDULE  2

Description  of the Project

The  objectives  of the  Project  are  to:  (a)  improve  the business environment  in  the
Borrower's territory; (b)  enhance the technological capabilities  of private sector enterprises
operating  in the  Borrower's territory;  and (c)  develop means  for  a public  dialogue among
the Borrower,  the private  sector, the  labor sector  and  society at  large  regarding  issues on
economic  competitiveness.

The Project  consists of the  following  parts,  subject to  such modifications  thereof

as the  Borrower  and  the Bank  may  agree  upon  from  time  to  time.

Part A: 

Business Environment  Enhancement

Provision of technical  assistance, and acquisition  of equipment required  therefor,

to:

(a) 

improve the business environment  in the Borrower's  territory, -including:
(i)  the  formulation  of  a  proposal  on  a  competition  law,  and  the  strengthening  of  the
Borrower's  institutional  capacity for the implementation  and enforcement  thereof;  (ii)  the
formulation  of a proposal to enhance  the Consumer Protection Law, and the strengthening
of the Borrower's  institutional  capacity  for  the implementation  and  enforcement  thereof;
(iii)  the  formulation  of  proposals  on  out-of-court  debt  restructuring;  and  (iv)  the
formulation  of a proposal  on a  bankruptcy  law,  and  the  strengthening  of the  Borrower's
institutional  capacity  for the  enforcement  thereof;

(b) 

coordinate,  supervise and  support the  establishment of the economic  and
legal  framework  for  the  infrastructure  services  required  for  the  improvement  of  the
business environment  in the Borrower's territory,  such as telecommunications,  power and
transportation, and customs services and for the privatization  of such services, as required;

(c) 

(i) modernize  the legal  framework  for trade and  commerce;  (ii)  promote
and  facilitate  foreign  trade  and  investment  and  strengthen  the  Borrower's  institutional
capacity  thereto;  (iii)  create  a registry  of movable  assets and  strengthen  the  Borrower's
institutional  capacity to operate  such registry;  and  (iv)  modernize  the commercial  and the
intellectual  property  registries  and  strengthening  of the  Borrower's  institutional  capacity
to operate  such  registries;  and

(d) 

modernize the Borrower's  financial system, through inter alia: (i)  reforms
for  the  internationalization  of  financial  services;  (ii)  policy  reforms  on  bank  failure
resolution  and deposit insurance,  and to define the role  of the Borrower  s central  bank  as

- 12  -

a  lender  of last  resort;  and  (iii) enhancement  of the  systems for  commercial  payments,
including:  cheque  clearing,  large  value  transfer  of funds  and  settlement  of  securities
trading.

Part B: 

Private  Sector  Technological  Capabilities  Enhancement

Provision  of technical  assistance,  and acquisition  of equipment  required  therefor,
to  enhance  the  technological  capabilities  of private  sector  enterprises  operating  in  the
Borrower's  territory,  including:  (a) the design  of a program  to  promote  the adoption  of
international  quality and productivity  standards; (b) the formulation  of a plan to strengthen
the  national  science  and  technology  institutions;  and  (c)  the  design  of a national  scheme
for demand-driven  technical  training.

Part  C: 

Public  Information

Provision of technical  assistance, and  acquisition  of equipment  required  therefor,
to  develop  a public  dialogue  on  economic  competitiveness  issues in  order  to,  inter  alia:
(a) enhance  public  awareness  on  the  objectives  of the  Borrower's  economic  and  social
reforms  and  the  instruments  to  reach  such  reforms;  and  (b)  improve  the  cooperation
between  the Borrower,  and  society at  large  to enhance  competitiveness  in the Borrower's
territory.
