WHEREAS  (A)  by  the  Loan  Agreement  dated  July  22,  1996,  as  amended  to  date  (the 
Loan Agreement) between the Borrower and the Bank, the Bank agreed to make available to the 
Borrower  an  amount  equal  to  fifty-nine  million  six  hundred  thousand  United  States  dollars

by the Project Agreement dated July 22, 1996 (the Project Agreement), between

25,  2000,  of  the  enactment  of  the  Uttar  Pradesh  Reorganization  Act,  creating  the  new  State  of

(D)

the Borrower has requested the Bank to amend the Loan Agreement to take into 

account the creation of Uttaranchal, and its obligation to carry out part of the Project.
