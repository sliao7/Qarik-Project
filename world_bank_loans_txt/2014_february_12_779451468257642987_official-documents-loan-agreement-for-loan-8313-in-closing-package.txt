The Project consists of the following parts: Component A: Highway Improvement Component 1. (a) Upgrading and rehabilitation of selected sections of state highways through, among other things, the financing of Input-Based Contracts; and (b) provision of support for land and resettlement and rehabilitation, tree cutting, utility relocation, environmental management, construction supervision, Project management and quality assurance services associated with this sub-component A. 1. (a) Upgrading a section of state highway to four-lane highway through the financing of a public-private partnership Annuity-Based DBFOMT Concession; (b) upgrading, operation and maintenance of a portion of the state highway network through the financing of an Output and Performance Based Contract; and (c) provision of support for land and resettlement and rehabilitation, tree cutting, utility relocation, environmental management, independent engineer services, Project management, and quality assurance services associated with this sub-component A.2. Component B: Sector Policy and Institutional Development l. Strengthening R&BD operational capacity including on: (a) design review, planning, road asset management, contract management, monitoring and evaluation, work zone and road safety, environment and social management, and grievance redress; (b) human resources strategies and management; and (c) information technology-based interventions. Strengthening Gujarat’s policy and planning capacity including: (a) updating the state’s policy and framework for road classification and responsibility; (6) development of a medium-term master plan for the development of the state road network; and (c) building capacity for better management of public-private partnerships contacts, road safety, and governance and accountability. Strengthening Gujarat’s capacity to better utilize the Engineering Staff College and Gujarat Engineering Research Institute; and to develop two centers of excellence in road and bridge design innovation, and construction management. Provision of support for implementation of the Governance and Accountability Action Plan.Component C: Road Safety Management Strengthening Gujarat’s road safety management system and improving its capacity to undertake multi-sectoral road safety interventions including: I. Provision of support for the implementation of multi-sectoral interventions on selected high volume, high risk road corridors to demonstrate road safety best practices. Strengthening the road safety module of GRMS and piloting integrated periodic maintenance and safety enhancement contracts. Strengthening GRMS including implementing policy changes on targeted high risk issues, establishment of a statewide road-accident database, provision of training to police on proactive road safety practices, development of a deterrence framework for unsafe driving behavior, preparation for a demonstration corridor program, and establishment of a state level lead agency for road safety management. Provision of support to the Road Safety Steering Committee in the implementation of and technical assistance for the above activities.SCHEDULE 2 Project Execution Section I.
