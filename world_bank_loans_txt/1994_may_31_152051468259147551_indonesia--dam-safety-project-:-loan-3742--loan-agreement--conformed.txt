Description of  the  Project

The  objective  of  the  Project  is 

to  reduce  the  risk  of  dam

failures  in  Indonesia.

The  Project  consists  of  the  following parts, subject  to  such
modifications  thereof  as  the  Borrower  and  the  Bank  may  agree  upon
from time  to  time to  achieve  such objectives:

PART A:  Dam Safety  Institutions

1. 

Establishment of  a  central  dam monitoring unit  in DGWRD.

2. 
Establishment  of  dam  monitoring  units  at  the  provincial  level,
and  establishment  of  dam  monitoring  units  for  the  Ministry  of  Public
Works'  Jatiluhur, Bengawan Solo,  and Brantas  dam schemes.

3. 
Provision  of  technical  assistance  to  the  Dam  Safety
Commission,  the  Dam  Safety  Unit,  and  the  units  referred  to  in
paragraphs  1  and  2  above,  and  provision  of  office  space,  office  and
field equipment, and transport to the  Dam Safety Unit  and the units
referred to  in  paragraphs  1 and  2  above.

4. 
Provision  of  training, workshops  and  seminars  to  members  of
the Dam Safety Commission and staff of  the Ministry of  Public Works
involved  in  the  dam  safety  program,  and  provision  of  related
equipment and materials.

PART B:  Basic Safety Facilities

Provision  of  the  following  basic  safety  facilities  and
services  to  dams  where  such  facilities  and  services  are  required,
including technical  assistance:

(a) 

Carrying  out  of  a  survey  of  the  dam  and  associated
structures 
detailed
inspection;

obtain  information  for 

to 

(b)  Provision of  communications  equipment and  facilities;

(c)  Provision  of  instrumentation  to  improve  recording  of
basic data relevant  to  the  safe operation of  the  dam;

-

(d) 

Clearing  of  the  dam  and  surrounding  area  to  allow
detailed  inspection  of  the  dam  and  associated
structures;  and

(e) 

Carrying out  of  emergency actions  to  ensure  the  safety
of  the  dam  and associated  structures.

PART C:  Remedial Works

Carrying  out  of  remedial  works  at  dams  where  detailed
inspections  have  identified  the  need  for  such  works  and  where
detailed  designs  have  been  completed,  including  provision  of
technical  assistance.
