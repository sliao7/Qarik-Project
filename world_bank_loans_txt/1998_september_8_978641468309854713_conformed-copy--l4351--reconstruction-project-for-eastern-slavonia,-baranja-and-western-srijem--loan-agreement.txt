SCHEDULE 2 

Description of the Project

The objectives of the Project are: (i) to repair and reconstruct critically 
needed water sector infrastructure to restart the local economy in the regions of 
Eastern Slavonia, Baranja and Western Srijem; (ii) to protect Kopacki Rit nature 
reserve from the possible adverse impact of adjacent agricultural activities; and 
(iii) clear Mines in the Project areas.
