The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives: Part A: Primary Education l. Acquisition and distribution of about 2,500,000 primary textbooks for the benefit of students in first, second, third, fourth, fifth and sixth grade of public primary schools. 2. Acquisition and distribution of: (i) about 3,250 teaching books during the first three years of Project implementation; and (ii) about 490 teaching books during each year following 1996 until the year 2000, for about 20 pedagogical libraries located within the Borrower’s territory. 3. Acquisition and distribution of about 317,400 books to about 811 student reading resource centers located in about 811 public primary schools (most of which will be located in UBN Areas), of which 158,700 books will be acquired and distributed during 1994 and the balance will be acquired and distributed between 1997 and the year 2000. 4. Acquisition and distribution of: (a) about 792 teaching material packages (each package consisting of: maps, pictures, blocks, audiovisuals, laboratory materials and a textbook maintenance kit) for public primary schools located in urban areas; (b) about 1,348 teaching material packages (each package consisting of: a dictionary, an atlas, maps, pictures, blocks and laboratory minisets) for public primary schools located in rural areas; and (c) about 120 teaching material packages (each package consisting of:      -~ 19 - maps, pictures, blocks, audiovisuals, laboratory materials and a textbook maintenance kit) for Educational Technological Centers. 5. Financing of about 800 Eligible Subprojects through the provision of Subgrants to Eligible Schools. 7.
