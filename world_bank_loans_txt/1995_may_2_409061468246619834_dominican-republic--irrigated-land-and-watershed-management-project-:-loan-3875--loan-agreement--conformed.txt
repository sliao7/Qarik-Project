The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives: Part A: Irrigation Infrastructure 1. | Completion of the construction of about 38 kilometers of the existing secondary and tertiary canals in the Nizao-Valdesia area. 2. Rehabilitation of about 17 kilometers of the existing secondary and tertiary canals in the Nizao-Valdesia area. 3. Construction of about 20 kilometers of elevated canals and canalettis in the Santiago area. 4. Improvement of the main canals and control structures in the Nizao-Valdesia and Santiago areas. 5. (a) Rehabilitation of about: (i) 42 kilometers of primary open drainage systems located within the Santiago area; and (ii) 37 kilometers of primary drainage systems located within the Ysura area; and (b) construction of about 31 kilometers of primary drainage systems located within the Ysura area. 6. Improvement of water distribution at the farm level, through land levelling of about: (a) 650 hectares in the Nizao-Valdesia areas (b) 3,200 hectares in the Ysura area; and (c) 3,400 hectares in the Santiago area. . 7. Installation of about 13 kilometers of drain pipes in the Ysura area.        - 19 . 8. Construction of two canal ditch protection works in the Santiago area. Part B: Nizao Watershed Management Systems Financing of Subprojects comprising: (a) forest land management programs; (b) agroforestry management programs; (c) rainfed and irrigated farming programs; and (d) supporting activities programs including, inter alia: (i) construction of off- farm small-irrigation infrastructure, including diversion and headwork structures; (ii) installation of pipelines and pressure tanks; and (iii) assistance to women’s groups engaged in the production of agricultural activities. Part C: Agricultural Development Carrying out of a research and development program on management of irrigated land, integrated pest and watershed, and environmental monitoring, for the benefit of about 5,000 small farmers in the Irrigated Area, and about 1,500 smail farmers in the Nizao Watershed Area. Part D: Institutional Development l. Provision of technical assistance to the Farmers Associations in the Project Area in order to strengthen their institutional capacity, through the implementation of a training program in the areas of administration, management of personnel, financing and accounting, operation and maintenance of irrigation systems, cost recovery procedures, and irrigation laws. 2. Provision of technical assistance to INDRHI in order to strengthen its institutional capacity for carrying out the Project, including the training of the staff required therefor. Part Es: Studies Carrying out of studies on: (a) canal regulation and Management of canal irrigation and drainage systems in the Project Area; (b) layout of the irrigation and drainage systems in the Project Area; (c) preparation of future agricultural projects; and (d) agricultural policy issues.        - 20 - Part.
