SCHEDULE 1 
 
Project Description 

 

The  objective  of  the  Project  is  to  introduce  international  standards  and  build 
long-term institutional capacity in the MoH and related healthcare institutions in support 
of key health sector reforms pursued by the Borrower in the context of the State Health 
Care Reform and Development Program. 
 

The Project consists of the following parts: 

 
Part A: Health Financing and Management 

Provision of technical assistance, goods, works, and training to: (i) improve the 
capacity for health policy and strategy formulation in the MoH; (ii) upgrade budgeting, 
planning, and purchasing capacity in the health sector; and (iii) strengthen the capacity of 
health  care  managers  as  well  as  improve  investment  planning  capacity  in  the  health 
sector.  
 
Part B: Health Care Quality Improvement

Provision of technical assistance, goods, works, and training to: (i) establish an 
accreditation  system  for  health  care  facilities;  (ii)  upgrade  clinical  practice  through  the 
development, review, and dissemination of clinical practice guidelines, and to introduce 
health technology assessment; (iii) reform the laboratory service through the development 
and  introduction  of  structural,  regulatory  and  infrastructure  planning  reforms;  and  (iv) 
reform  the  blood  transfusion  service  through  the  development  and  introduction  of 
structural and regulatory and infrastructure planning reforms.  
 
Part C: Reform of Medical Education and Science

Provision  of  technical  assistance,  goods,  works,  and  training  to:  (i)  reform 
undergraduate  and  continuing  medical  education,  including  through  a  review  of  the 
medical education system, improvement of admissions regulations, the creation of quality 
assurance  system,  establishment  of  a  training  system  in  evidence-based  medicine, 
development of a program for reform of post-diploma medical education and continuous 
professional  development,  improvement  of  certification  and  licensing  systems,  and 
improvement of the medical education financing system; and (ii) upgrade the medical and 
health science research system. 
 
Part D: Health Information System Development 

Provision  of  technical  assistance,  goods,  works,  and  training  to  develop  and 
deploy  an  integrated  health  information  system  for  patient,  resource  and  financial 
management. 

5

Part E: Pharmaceutical Policy Reform 

Provision of technical assistance, goods, works, and training to strengthen drug 
policy, including through a restructuring of the benefit system, the introduction of price 
control  methods,  a  review  of  counterfeit  and  substandard  drug  proliferation,  the 
establishment of institutional mechanisms to promote rational prescribing, and evidence-
based analysis to remove outdated and unreliable drugs from the market. 

 

Part F: Food Safety and WTO Accession 

Provision  of  technical  assistance,  goods,  works,  and  training  to  develop  and 

introduce a system for assurance of food safety in compliance with WTO requirements. 
 
Part G: Project Management 

Provision  of  technical  assistance,  goods,  works,  and  training  to  support  Project 

coordination, implementation and management.
