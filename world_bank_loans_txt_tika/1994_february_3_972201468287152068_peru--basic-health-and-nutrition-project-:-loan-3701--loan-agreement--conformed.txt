AGREEMENT, dated APRIL S , 1994, between

WHEREAS the Borrower, having satisfied itself as to the

ARTICLE I 

General Conditions; Definitions 

Section 1.01. The "General Conditions Applicable to Loan and 

Guarantee Agreements" of the Bank, dated January 1, 1985, with the 

modifications set forth below (the General Conditions) constitute 

an integral part of this Agreement: © 

(a) The last sentence of Section 3.02 is deleted. 

(b) In Section 6.02, sub-paragraph (k) is re-lettered as 
sub-paragraph (1) and a new sub-paragraph (k) is added to read: 

"(k) An extraordinary situation shall have arisen 

under which any further withdrawals under the Loan would be 
inconsistent with the provisions of Article III, Section 3 

of the Bank’s Articles of Agreement.” 

Section 1.02. Unless the context otherwise requires, the 
several terms defined in the General Conditions and in the 
Preamble to this Agreement have the respective meanings therein 

set forth and the following additional terms have the following 
meanings: 

(a) "Communications Services" means the development, 

production and distribution of printed, audio-visual and 

educational materials, and the design and execution of mass media 
campaigns; 

   



  

  

(b) "Community Health Worker" means any one of a variety 

of health promotion agents in the community, including health 

promoters, traditional birth attendants and child growth monitors, 

provided that: (i) they have been identified and selected by their 

communities; (ii) they have been active as health care providers 

in the community prior to initiation of the Project; (iii) they 

serve as advocates of their communities vis-a-vis the formal 

health care system rather than as extension agents of the formal 
health care system; and (iv) any compensation they receive is the 

responsibility of the community to determine and provide; 

(c) "Cooperative Agreement" means a contract between the 

Ministry of Health and a non-governmental organization for 
cooperation in matters of mutual interest but without payment of 

the latter by the former; 

(d) "Health Care Facility" means a fully-built facility 

for the provision of health services that is staffed with full- 
time personnel, registered by the Ministry of Health, and open to 

the public; 

(e) "Health Center" means a Health Care Facility which 
services a population of between approximately 10,000 and 60,000, 
has at least one full-time physician on its staff, and provides at 
least the following services in addition to those available at a 

Health Post: simple deliveries, treatment of severe diarrhea and 
respiratory infections; 

(£) "Health Post" means a Health Care Facility which 

services a population of between approximately 2,000 and 10,060, 
has at least one full-time formally trained nurse or health 
auxiliary worker on its staff, and provides at least the following 
services: provision of vitamins, minerals, antibiotics and 
immunizations, and treatment of mild diarrhea and respiratory 

infections; 

(g) "Ministry" means the Borrower’s Ministry of Health; 

(hk) "National Plan for Breastfeeding and Weaning" means 
the Ministry’s plan of the same name agreed with the Bank for the 

purpose of Part D of the Project; 

(i) "Project Area" means districts and provinces selected 
by the Borrower and accepted by the Bank for inclusion in the 
Project;    



  

  

(3) "Service Contract" means a contract between the 

Ministry of Health and a non-governmental organization for the 

supply of services by the latter paid for by the former; 

(k) "Sub-regional Directors of Health Services" means the 

Ministry’s sub-regional Directors of Health Services in the 

Project Area, including the Director of Medical Services for Lima; 

(1) "Special Account" means the account referred to in 

Section 2.02 (b) of this Agreement; and 

(m) "year" means calendar year. 

ARTICLE II 

The Loan 

Section 2.01. The Bank agrees to lend to the Borrower, on 

the terms and conditions set forth or referred to in the Loan 
Agreement, various currencies that shall have an aggregate value 
equivalent to the amount of thirty-four million dollars 

($34,000,000), being the sum of withdrawals of the proceeds of the 
Loan, with each withdrawal valued by the Bank as of the date of 
such withdrawal. 

Section 2.02. (a) The amount of the Loan may be withdrawn 

from the Loan Account in accordance with the provisions of 
Schedule 1 to this Agreement for expenditures made (or, if the 

Bank shall so agree, to be made) in respect of the reasonable cost 
of goods and services required for the Project described in 
Schedule 2. to this Agreement and to be financed out of the 

proceeds of the Loan. 

(b) The Borrower shall, for the purposes of the Project, 

open and maintain in dollars a special deposit account in a 

commercial bank on terms and conditions acceptable to the Bank, 
including appropriate protection against set-off, seizures or 

attachment. Deposits into, and payments out of, the Special 

Account shall be made in accordance with the provisions of 

Schedule 5 to this Agreement. 

Section 2.03. The Closing Date shall be June 30, 2000 or 

such later date as the Bank shall establish. The Bank shall 

promptly notify the Borrower of such later date. 

   



  

  

Section 2.04. The Borrower shall pay to the Bank a 

commitment charge at the rate of three-fourths of one percent (3/4 

of 1%) per annum on the principal amount of the Loan not withdrawn 
from time to time.

principal amount of the Loan withdrawn and outstanding from time 
to time, at a rate for each Interest Period equal to the Cost of 
Qualified Borrowings determined in respect of the preceding

dates specified in Section 2.06 of this Agreement, the Borrower 
shall pay interest accrued on the principal amount outstanding 
during the preceding Interest Period, calculated at the rate 

applicable during such Interest Period. 

(b) As soon as practicable after the end of each Semester, 

the Bank shall notify the Borrower of the Cost of Qualified 
Borrowings determined in respect of such Semester. 

(c) For the purposes of this Section: 

(i) "Interest Period" means a six-month period 
ending on the date immediately preceding each 
date specified in Section 2.06 of this 
Agreement, beginning with the Interest Period 

in which this Agreement is signed. 

(ii) "Cost of Qualified Borrowings" means the cost, 

as reasonably determined by the Bank and 

expressed as a percentage per annum, of the

after June 30, 1982, excluding such borrowings

to fund: (A) the Bank’s investments; and (B) 

loans which may be made by the Bank after 
July 1, 1989 bearing interest rates determined 
otherwise than as provided in paragraph (a) of 
this Section. 

(iii) "Semester" means the first six months or the 

second six months of a calendar year.

SCHEDULE 2 

Description of the Project 

The objective of the Project is to improve the health and 
nutritional status in the Project Area, particularly among poor 
women and chiidren. 

The Project consists of tre following parts, subject to such 
modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon 

from time to time to achieve such objective: 

Part A: Maternal and Child Health Care 

Improvement in the utilization and quality of maternal and 
child health care services in the Project Area, with particular 
emphasis on preventive care in the areas of reproductive health 
and the health of infants and young children, including the 
provision and use of appropriate medicines, medical supplies and 
equipment, and vehicles. 

Part B: Nutrition 

l. Improvement of the nutritional status of children under 

three years of age in the Project Area, particularly through 
expansion of community-based child growth monitoring, close 
follow-up of growth problems, and the distribution of 
micronutrient supplements and antihelmintics. 

2. Carrying out of operations research and other studies in the 

following general areas: (a) accuracy of recording and 
interpreting child weights and counseling skills; (b) alternative 

technologies for growth monitoring; and (c) development of 
innovative strategies and programs (including pilot projects) for 

strengthening maternal and child nutrition in rural areas with 
potential for cost-effective replication. 

Part C: Tuberculosis Treatment 

Improvement of detection and treatment practices for tuber- 
culosis by Community Health Workers in the Project Area, including 

the provision and use of testing equipment and medicine. 

  

 



  

  

-17 - 

Part D: Information, Education, Communications and Training 

Implementation within the Project Area of: (a) the National 

Plan for Breastfeeding and Weaning through the use of mass media, 
community participation and inter-personal communication, and 
execution of similar activities for maternal and child health care 

and nutrition; (b) training programs for Community Health Workers 
and other local health providers to enhance knowledge of 
appropriate health and nutrition practices; and (c) an impact 
study on the activities carried out under the Project.
