SCHEDULE 2 

Description of the Project 

The objectives of the Project are to assist the Borrower in: (a) increasing the 
international competitiveness of small and medium enterprises operating in its territory; 
and (b) improving the performance of export-related agencies of the Borrower, so as to 
facilitate export growth. 

The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof 
as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such 
objectives: 

Part A: Enterprise Assistance Program 

1. Establishment and operation of a grant scheme, on a cost-sharing basis, to help 
finance expenditures incurred by enterprises in the preparation and execution of Export | 
Plans. 

2. Preparation, publication and dissemination of a directory of export support | 
services providers worldwide. 

Part B: Institutional Strengthening 

1. Provision of technical assistance to ANA, DGI, IASCAV, INAL, INTI, SENASA 

and SDOE aimed at the simplification of procedures so as to reduce the time involved in 
processing import and export orders, and acquisition of the equipment required therefor. 

2. Provision of technical assistance to INTI, IRAM and selected private laboratories, 
aimed at improving quality control certification services for exports, and acquisition of 
the equipment required therefor. 

3. Provision of technical assistance to UCPFE, including policy formulation in the 
area of export promotion and acquisition of appropriate equipment. 

4. Provision of technical assistance to Fundacién Exportar with respect to 
commercial promotion activities. 

* * * 

The Project is expected to be completed by January 31, 1998.

(ii) such services shall be used exclusively in the carrying out of the 
Export Plan;
