SCHEDULE 1 

Project Description 

The objective of the Project is to reduce the vulnerability of fishing communities to 
extreme weather events in selected counties in Fujian Province. 

The Project consists of the following parts: 

Part A: Construction and Upgrading of Fishing Port Facilities 

1. Construction, rehabilitation and/or upgrading of fishing port facilities in selected counties 
in Fujian Province including, inter alia, breakwaters, berthing facilities, embankments, 
land formations, trestle bridges, anchorages, access roads and port cottages, as well as 
emergency shelters and port management cottages ashore. 

Part B: Improvement of the Early Warning and Response System 

I. Development and setting up of an early warning and emergency response plan for Xia’ pu 
County, including comprehensive lists of actions to be carried out at different typhoon 
warning levels by local authorities and fishery communities. 

2. Acquisition of suitable hardware, software and communications systems necessary to 
support the operation of Xia’pu County’s Emergency Response and Command Centre, as 
well as the procurement of a rescue vessel and a rescue vehicle for emergency rescue 
operations. 

Part C: Training and Capacity Building 

1. Supporting in-service training to local fishermen by, inter alia, the organization and 
carrying out of training activities (including domestic and international study trips) aimed 
at improving FOTS administrators’ and teachers’ capacities on school management, 
advanced training on fishing, curricula development, teaching reform and project 
management. 

2. Developing and updating FOTS curricula and textbooks for fishermen in training, 
including the design and delivery of specialized training programs and a database of 
capacity-building activities for fishermen. 

3. - Provision of support to FOTS for: (a) the construction, rehabilitation and/or upgrading of 
in-campus teaching buildings, dormitories, and supporting training facilities; and (b) 
procurement of training equipment, books and learning materials.



Part D: Technical Support and Project Management 

Provision of technical assistance and project management support to Fujian PPMO and 
Xia’pu PMO through, inter alia: (a) the carrying out of training activities and delivery of 
technical assistance to staff of Fujian PPMO and Xia’pu PMO, including design and 
procurement documentation review, and preparation of progress reports; (b) the carrying 
out of domestic and overseas study trips; (c) development of the Port Operational! and 
Maintenance Manual to enhance routine fishing ports’ management capacity and 
support of Project activities; and (d) conducting independent external monitoring of 
RAP and EMP implementation.
