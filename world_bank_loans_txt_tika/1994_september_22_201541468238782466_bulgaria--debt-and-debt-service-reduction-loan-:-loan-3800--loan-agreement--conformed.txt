REPUBLIC OF BULGARIA (the Borrower) and INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR 
RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (the Bank). 

_ WHEREAS (A) on July 28, 1994, the Borrower implemented its 

debt and debt service reduction agreements (the "DDSR Agreements") 
with Participating Lenders (as hereinafter defined) for its debt 

relief and debt service reduction program described in the 
Borrower’s 1994 Financing Plan (the "1994 Financing Plan"), designed 
to support the Borrower’s medium term macro-economic objectives and 
policies, as a result of which the Borrower: (i) purchased for cash 
the equivalent of approximately US$798,300,000 of Eligible Debt (as 
hereinafter defined) with a disccunt of 74.81252; (ii) issued Front 
Loaded Interest Reduction Bonds due 2012 (the "FLIRBs") in exchange 
for the equivalent of approximately US$1,657,500,000 of Eligible 
Debt (as hereinafter defined); (iii) issued Discount Bonds due 2024 

(the "Discount Bonds") in exchange for the equivalent of 

approximately US$3,730,400,000 of Eligible Debt (as hereinafter 
defined); and (iv) paid the equivalent of US$63,800,000 in Past Due 

Interest (PDI) on Eligible Debt (as hereinafter defined); purchased 

for cash the equivalent of approximately US$223,300,000 of PDI on 
Eligible Debt (as hereinafter defined); and issued PDI Bonds in 
exchange for the equivalent of US$1,614,000 in PDI on Eligible Debt 

(as hereinafter defined); 

(B) the Borrower has requested the assistance of the Bank 
and the International Monetary Fund in the financing of a portion 
of the cost of: (i) the purchase price of Eligible Debt (as 

hereinafter defined) purchased by the Borrower under the 1994 
Financing Plan; and (ii) the acquisition of collateral to secure 
principal and interest payments on the Discount Bonds and the 
interest payments on the FLIRBs; and 

WHEREAS the Bank has agreed, on the basis, inter alia, of the 
foregoing, to extend the Loan to the Borrower upon the terms and 
conditions set forth in this Agreement; 

NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:

ARTICLE I 

General Conditions; Definitions 

Section 1.01. The "General Conditions Applicable to Loan and 
Guarantee Agreements" of the Bank, dated January 1, 1985, with the 

modifications set ferth in Schedule 4 to this Agreement (the General 
Conditions), constitute an integral part of this Agreement. 

Section 1.02. Unless the context otherwise requires, the 

several terms defined in the General Conditions and in the Preamble 
to this Agreement have the respective meanings therein set forth and 
the following additional terms have the following meanings: 

(a) "Debt Purchase Offer" means the debt purchase offer made 

by the Borrower under the agreements entered into pursuant to the 
1994 Financing Plan; 

(b) “Eligible Debt" has the same meaning ascribed thereto 
in the 1994 Financing Plan; | 

(c) "Fiscal Year" means the fiscal year of the Borrower, 
which begins on January 1 and ends on December 31 of each year; 

(d) "Participating Lenders" means the commercial banks and 
ether financial institutions with Eligible Debt that have 
participated in the 1994 Financing Plan; and 

(e) "Purchase" means the aggregate of cash payments made by 

the Borrower to Participating Lenders under the agreements entered 
into pursuant to the Debt Purchase Offer and to be partially 
financed out of the proceeds of the Loan; and "Specific Purchase" 

means the specific portions of the Purchase to be financed out of 
the proceeds of the Loan in accordance with Schedule 1 to this 
Agreement. :

The Loan

terms and conditions set forth or referred to in this Agreement, 
various currencies that shall have an aggregate value equivalent to

SCHEDULE 1 

Withdrawal of the Proceeds of the Loan 

l. Subject to the provisions set forth or referred to in this 
Schedule, the proceeds of the Loan may be withdrawn from the Loan 
Account for expenditures made in respect of the Purchase to be 
financed out of such proceeds. 

2. Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph 1 above, rio 
withdrawals shall be made: (a) in respect of payments made for 
expenditures prior to July 27, 1994; and (b) in respect of any 
Specific Purchase, unless the Borrower has furnished to the Bank a 
certificate in form and substance satisfactory to the Bank showing 
that the Specific Purchase in question, as provided for in the 
corresponding agreement or agreements relating to Part B of the 
Project, remains in full force and effect. Without limitation or 
restriction on the provisions of Section 5.03 of the General 
Conditions, if the Bank shall so request in connection with one or 
more withdrawals from the Loan Accecunt, the Borrower shall furnish

counsel acceptable to the Bank in connection with any of the 
aforesaid conditions.

SCHEDULE 2 

Description of the Project 

The objective of the Project is to support the Borrower’s 1994 
Financing Plan. 

The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such 
modifications thereto as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon 
from time to time to achieve such objectives: 

Part As 

Provision of Collateral for the purpose of securing the 
payment of principal and interest on the Discount Bonds and the 
payment of interest on FLIRBs. 

Part B: 

Purchase of Eligible Debt by the Borrower pursuant to one or 
more agreements entered into pursuant to the Debt Purchase Offer.
