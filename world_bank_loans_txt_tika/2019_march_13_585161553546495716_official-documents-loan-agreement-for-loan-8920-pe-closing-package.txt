SCHEDULE 1 

Project Description 

The objectives of the Project are to: (i) improve the resolutive capacity and quality of public First- 
Level Health Services in Lima Metropolitan Area and Prioritized Regions; and (ii) increase the 
capacity of the Single Health Information System and the public sector’s pharmaceutical products 
and medical supplies provision system. 

The Project consists of the following parts: 

Part 1: Improving the Organization and Supply of Health Services Using an Integrated 

Health Networks (RIS) Model in Lima Metropolitan Area and Prioritized Regions 

(Components 1 and 2 of the RIS Program) 

Provision of support to introduce a new First-Level Health Services delivery model in Lima 
Metropolitan Area and Prioritized Regions, through, infer alia: 

(a) Design, implementation tools, dissemination, training, and monitoring of the 
Integrated Health Networks model to respond to the new patterns of the burden of disease, 
including clinical practice guidelines, patient care pathways, determination of infrastructure, 
equipment, personnel and service standards, organizational, management, and financing 
arrangements. 

(b) (i) Mapping of health facilities; (ii) formulation of strategic plans in terms of health 
service needs, health service provision, health infrastructure, medical and non-medical 

equipment, connectivity and personnel; (iii) determination of the requirements for 
connectivity, IT infrastructure and equipment under a network approach; and (iv) 
identification of optimal locations for the rehabilitation, expansion, reconstruction or 

construction of First-Level Health Facilities. 

(c) Design and implementation of: (i) a plan to strengthening the health service human 
resources capacity; (ii) change management and communication plans; and (iii) monitoring 
and evaluation of the Integrated Health Networks model. 

(d) Carrying out of works for construction, refurbishment, renovation, expansion, 

relocation and tear-down/reconstruction of new or existing First-Level Health Facilities to 
serve as “Up-to-12 Hour IPRESS” and “24 Hour IPRESS”. 

(e) Provision of equipment, including medical and non-medical equipment, for the 
‘“Up-to-12 Hour IPRESS” and “24 Hour IPRESS”.



Part 2: Improving the Capacity of the Single Health Information System (SUIS) at the 
National Level (Component 3 of the RIS Program) 

Provision of support to generate adequate access to, and management information for, the clinical 
and administrative decision-making process in the Borrower’s health system, through, inter alia: 

(a) Strengthening of governance and standardization of data and ICT processes. 

(b) Provision of technological and network infrastructure to support the 
implementation of the SUIS, including: (i) carrying out of works for the construction of 
three buildings in the cities of Lima, Trujillo and Huancavelica to install data centers to 
ensure storage, processing, security and data availability; and (ii) acquisition of equipment 
and carrying out of works to provide internal and external connectivity to First-Level 
Health Facilities in Lima Metropolitan Area and Prioritized Regions. 

(c) Strengthening of health personnel capacity for the registration, management and 
use of information in the SUIS. 

(d) Design, promotion and dissemination of user engagement instruments for the 
SUIS. 

Part 3: Improving the Management of Pharmaceutical Products and Medical Supplies in 
Lima Metropolitan Area and Prioritized Regions (Component 5 of the RIS Program) 

Provision of support to improve access in terms of quantity, quality, timeliness and affordability to 
pharmaceutical products and medical supplies in public health facilities in Lima Metropolitan Area 
and Prioritized Regions through, inter alia: 

(a) (i) improvement of the management model; (ii) improving of the financing model; 
and (iii) improvement of the listing, planning, procurement, storage, distribution and use 
processes. 

(b) | Support the implementation of a traceability and stock management system for 
pharmaceutical products and medical supplies (including medical devices). 

(c) | Carrying out of works for the construction and rehabilitation of, and provision of 
equipment and vehicles for: (i) a new central warehouse in the city of Lima; and (ii) 
selected warehouses in Prioritized Regions. 

Part 4: Project Management 

Provision of support to MINSA for Project management, monitoring and implementation, 
acquisition of equipment and provision of technical assistance in the areas of contract management, 
procurement, financial management, technical and monitoring and evaluation (including Project 
audits).
