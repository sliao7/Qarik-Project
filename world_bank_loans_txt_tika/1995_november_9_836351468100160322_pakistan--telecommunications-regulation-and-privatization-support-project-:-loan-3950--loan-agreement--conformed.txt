SCHEDULE 2 

Description of the Project | 

The objective of the Project is to assist the Borrower in the efficient develo,:ment 
and privatization of its telecommunications sector. 

The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof 
as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such 
objectives: 

Part A: Radio Spectrum Management 

Acquisition and installation of an mtegrated radio spectrum management and 
‘ monitoring system providing both administrative and technical facilities. 

Part B: Institutional Development 

1. Provision of technical assistance to the Authority and the Board in establishing 
appropriate organizational structures and strengthening technical capabilities to carry out 
their respective regulatory functions, including acquisition of relatec: equipment. 

2. Training of the staff of the Authority and the Board. 

Part C: Privatization Support 

Carrying out of a study of the telecommunications sector reform issues to assist 
in the establishment of suitable strategies regarding tariffs, demand management, quality 
of services and other related issues to support the liberalization and privatization of the 
telecommunications sector. 

The Project is expected to be completed by December 31, 1998.
