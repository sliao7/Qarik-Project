ARTICLE I — GENERAL CONDITIONS; DEFINITIONS 

1.01. The General Conditions (as defined in the Appendix to this Agreement) apply to 
and form part of this Agreement. 

1.02. Unless the context requires otherwise, the capitalized terms used in this Agreement

ARTICLE Ii — LOAN 

2.01. The Bank agrees to lend to the Borrower the amount of three hundred and twenty

SCHEDULE 1 

Project Description 

The objectives of the Project are to provide temporary income support to poor 
households in selected areas of Angola, and to strengthen the delivery mechanisms for a 
permanent social safety net system. 

The Project consists of the following parts: 

1. Cash Transfer program to poor households 

(a) 

(b) 

(c) 

(i) Development of the following delivery mechanisms for social 
protection programs: unique social registry of poor and vulnerable 
households, and management information system for safety net programs, 
including household targeting, payment, monitoring and evaluation, 
grievance mechanism information and complaint redress, and household 
referral to productive inclusion and human development programs; and (ii) 
provision of capacity building on the implementation of the unique social 
registry and management information system for safety nets developed 
under Part 1(a)(i). 

Implementation of the following delivery mechanisms developed under 
Part 1(a)(i) of the Project, in the Participating Municipalities: unique social 
registry of poor and vulnerable households, and management information 
system for safety nets. 

(i) Provision of Cash Transfers to Beneficiaries; (ii) support for the 

issuance of identification documents to women Beneficiaries; (iii) 
provision of capacity building to Beneficiaries on the management of the 
Cash Transfers. 

2. Development of a safety nets system 

(a) 

(b) 

(i) Development and implementation of a communication strategy for the 

Borrower’s planned subsidy reform; and (ii) development and 
implementation of information, education and communication strategies 
for the Cash Transfers and other selected social safety net initiatives. 

(i) Provision of technical assistance to selected Beneficiaries to increase 

their income-generating capacity, including inter alia, financial 

management skills and numeracy; (ii) provision of Productive Inclusion 

Grants to selected Beneficiaries to carry out income generation activities; 
(iii) provision of technical assistance on strengthening coordination 
between social sectors and on improving access of selected Beneficiaries



to human development services; and (iv) provision of Block Grants to 
CASIs for selected activities for strengthening linkages between 
Beneficiaries and human development services in Participating 
Municipalities, including, inter alia: mobile identification and registration 
campaigns, behavior change campaigns, health fairs, support to victims of 
domestic violence and social inclusion of teenagers and youth, Training 
and Workshops, and equipment required for the purpose, including 
maintenance costs. 

3. Project management 

(a) 

(b) 

Provision of support for the implementation of the Project, including the 
carrying out of Project management, administration, coordination, 
financial management, procurement, monitoring and evaluation, social 
and environment-related activities. 

Provision of capacity building on strategic planning, fund raising, 
advocacy for social assistance and addressing gender gaps in human 
capital and economic empowerment.
