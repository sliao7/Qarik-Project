SCHEDULE 2 

Description of the Project 

The objectives of the Project are: (a) to improve the internal and external 

efficiency, quality and equity of the educational services provided by the MS-Schools 
and PSP-Schools; and (b) to strengthen the managerial capacity in the Borrower’s 
secondary education sector. 

The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof 
as the Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such 
objectives: 

Part A: Improvement of the internal and external efficiency, quality and equity of the 
educational services provided by MS-Schools and PSP-Schools 

1. Establishment and operation of a curriculum planning and evaluation unit, including 
the provision of technical assistance to such unit in support of its functions. 

2. Redesign of the current subject-based core curriculum for secondary education in the 
Borrower’s territory. 

3. Design of teaching and learning prototype materials based, inter alia, on the redesigned 
curriculum referred to in Part A.2 above, including the production and distribution of such 
materials to interested MS-Schools and PSP-Schools. 

4. Provision of technical assistance to all MS-Schools and PSP-Schools which request 

MINEDUC or TSN to assist such schools, inter alia, in: (a) the redefinition of their 

complementary curriculum and syllabus; (b) the provision of in-service teacher training 
referred to in Parts A.9, A.11 and A.19 below; and (c) the carrying out of internal and 
external evaluation of students referred to in Parts A.6 and A.8 below. 

5. Carrying out of activities to inform interested schools and the public on educational 
innovations by MS-Schools and PSP-Schools. 

6. Carrying out of: (a) external evaluations of students attending the twelfth grade in 
secondary schools within the Borrower’s territory; (b) a general skill certification of 
students attending the tenth grade in secondary schools within the Borrower’s territory; 
and (c) a specific skill certification of students attending the twelfth grade in T-VOC 
Secondary Schools. 

7. Production of student certification sets and student certification manuals, including the 

  
 



  

  

-17- 

distribution of such sets and manuals to T-VOC Secondary Schools. 

8. Provision of in-service training to teachers of MS-Schools and PSP-Schools in order 
to enable such teachers to carry out internal evaluations of the students attending such 
schools. 

9. Design of extra-curricular activities for at-risk students (with respect to social and
