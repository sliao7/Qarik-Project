ARTICLE I - GENERAL CONDITIONS; DEFINITIONS

1.01, The General Conditions (as defined in the Appendix to this Agreement) apply to and form

1.02. Unless the context requires otherwise, the capitalized terms used in this Agreement have
the meanings ascribed to them in the General Conditions or in the Appendix to this
Agreement.

ARTICLE H - LOAN

the project described in Schedule I to this Agreement ("Project").

2.02. The Borrower may withdraw the proceeds of the Loan in accordance with Section III of
Schedule 2 to this Agreement.

following a Conversion; subject to Section 3.02(e) of the General Conditions.

2.06. The Payment Dates are June 15 and December 15 in each year.

Section) for each said day ("Exposure Surcharge"). The Exposure Surcharge (if any) shall

(b) For purposes of this Section the following terms have the meanings set forth below:

SCHEDULE 1

Project Description

The objective of the Project is to enhance agricultural productivity, profitability and climate
resilience of smallholder farmers in selected districts of Andhra Pradesh.

The Project consists of the following parts:

Part A. Improving Irrigated Agriculture Efficiency

A. I Institutional Strengthening and Capacity Building of WUAs

Capacity building of Water User Associations (WUAs) for better operation and
maintenance of tank systems and better water management, through training, technical
assistance, support from private operator, and establishment of service feedback
mechanism and user satisfaction surveys.

A.2 Rehabilitation and Modernization of Small-Scale Community-Based Irrigation Systems

Modernization of irrigation infrastructure of tank systems located in selected cascades.

A.3 Improving Water Productivity and Efficiency

1. Improvement of water efficiency and productivity, and promotion of conjunctive
water use in the tank command and its zone of influence, by, inter alia: (a)
establishing cascade monitoring and decision support systems; (b) installing censor-
based measuring devices to monitor water discharge to farms; and (c) broadening
stakeholder participation in planning and managing water resources.

2. Promotion of community-led management to improve groundwater conditions.

Part B. Promoting Climate-Smart Agricultural Practices

B. I Support to Climate-Smart Crop Production and Diversification

I. Support for technology dissemination and transfer for sustainable and climate-
resilient crop intensification.

2. Support for crop diversification through, inter alia: (a) mobilization and organization
of targeted farmers into farmer producer organizations for cooperative production and
produce marketing; (b) market demand assessments for profitable crops; (c)
demonstration of new crops and their production technologies; (d) rejuvenation of old
orchards; and (e) strengthening of access to seeds and other inputs for new crops.



-5-

3. Support for mechanization of farm activities, including establishment of farm
machinery centers to provide machinery and other equipment on lease and to provide
necessary support for their usage.

B.2 Support to Climate-Smart Aquaculture

Improvement of aquaculture production and profitability, including through: (a) support
for aquaculture management approaches; (b) improved access to good quality fish seed and
feed; (c) production of high value species; (d) dissemination of improved production
technology; (e) strengthening of extension-farmer linkages; (f) biosafety and disease
surveillance; (g) improvement and management of fish marketing infrastructure; (h) value
addition; and (i) support for compliance with food safety standards.

Part C: Post-Harvest Management, Market and Agribusiness Promotion

Enhancement of farmers' profitability by improving their access to markets and their
capacity to add value to their produce, including through: (1) easing market infrastructure
constraints on processing, storage, handling and marketing of farm produce; and (2)
facilitating linkages of farmer producer organizations and value chain operators to local,
national and international markets.

Part D: Project Management and Capacity Building

Support for Project implementation, monitoring and reporting.

to under Section I.A.2 of the Schedule to the Project Agreement.

.7. "ESMF" means the Environmental and Social Management Framework , prepared by the
