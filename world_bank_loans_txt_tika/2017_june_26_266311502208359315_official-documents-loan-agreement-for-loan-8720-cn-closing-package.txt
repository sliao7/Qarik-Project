SCHEDULE 1

Project Description

The objective of the Project is to improve the quality and relevance of Project
Schools in Gansu and strengthen the school and industry linkage.

The Project consists of the following parts:

Part A. Strengthen the Demand-Driven TVET System

1. Promote the coordination and management of TVET through the piloting of a skills
council, as an advisory board to provide select skills' demand information in
Lanzhou New District, and provision of support to the Project Leading Group to
organize campus visits and report on the Project's implementation progress.

2. Promote provincial policy development through the carrying out of evidence-
based policy studies on selected key topics related to TVET.

3. Provision of support to strengthen and institutionalize the linkages between
schools and industry by, inter alia: (a) establishing or improving School-Industry
Collaboration Committees and sector specific Technical Advisory Committees; (b)
piloting new models of school-industry cooperation based on inputs from the
school-industry collaboration and the characteristics of Project Schools; and (c)
evaluating school-industry collaboration.

Part B. Strengthen TVET Delivery through Comprehensive School Reforms

1. Improving management and instructional capacity in delivering quality training
programs through, inter alia: (a) preparing professional development plans for
teachers and administrators, and setting up incentive mechanisms for high
performance; and (b) provision of domestic and overseas training, and organizing
various capacity-building programs targeting several cohorts of teachers and
administrators.

2. Carrying out of activities aimed - at updating the curriculum, pedagogy and
assessment at Project Schools.

3. Enhancing school management through, inter alia: (a) provision of technical
assistance to support the drafting or updating of school charters and other relevant
regulations; (b) conducting school management diagnosis to streamline
management and personnel arrangements, and establishing a digitalized
management system; and (c) sharing experience on quality and relevance
improvements with selected Partner Schools.



-7-

4. Expansion and upgrading of facilities through, inter alia: (a). construction oftraining centres within selected Project Schools; (b) providing key trainingequipment to support quality training programs; and (c) developing schoolguidelines on the management of training facilities and equipment.

Part C. Project Management and Monitoring and Evaluation

1. Facilitating Project management through the carrying out of capacity buildingactivities for Project Management Office (PMO) and Project Implementation Unit(PIU) staff, and support knowledge dissemination activities at the provincial level.
2. Provision of technical assistance to support the monitoring and evaluation of theProject activities, including the carrying out of an impact evaluation.
