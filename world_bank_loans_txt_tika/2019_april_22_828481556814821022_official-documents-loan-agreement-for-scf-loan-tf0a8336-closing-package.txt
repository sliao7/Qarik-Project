SCHEDULE 1 

Project Description 

The objective of the Project is to increase electricity generation capacity from 
renewable energy mini-grids in selected areas by mobilizing ESCOs. 

The Project consists of the following parts: 

Part 1: Credit Facility to Support Renewable Energy Mini-grid Subprojects 

Provisions of Sub-loans by the AEPC to PBs to be utilized as a line of credit for providing 
the long-term financing to the ESCOs. 

Part 2: Technical Assistance to the Mini-grid Sector, ESCOs and Partner Banks and

Project Management Support. 

Support of the PMT and enhancement of AEPC’s capacity as a credit wholesaler. 

Training and Capacity Development. 

Provision of capacity building and training on, among others: (i) strengthening of 
coordination and oversight roles for the AEPC and MoEWRI; (ii) preparation and 
modification of guidelines and policies to promote mini-grid sector; (iii) business 
model development and optimization, technology selection, quality control and 
management, operation and maintenance, tariff management, project financing for 
ESCOs; (iv) credit due diligence, monitoring of loan performance and risk 

assessment for PBs; and (iv) procurement, financial management, environmental 

and social assessment and management of AEPC, PBs, ESCOs and industry 

service providers supporting the Subprojects. 

Preparation of Studies and Key Project Documents. 

(i) Preparation of feasibility studies, technical design, environmental and social 

assessments and bidding documents of Subprojects.

to, instruments and model agreements among AEPC, ESCOs and PBs.
